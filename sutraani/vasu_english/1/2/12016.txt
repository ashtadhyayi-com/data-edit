Thus उपायत कन्याम् or उपायंस्त कन्याम् 'he espoused the girl,' उपायत or उपायंस्त भार्याम् 'he espoused his wife.'

The word '<i>Upayamana</i>' in the <i>sutra</i> means 'to accept in marriage,' 'to espouse.' The verb यम् preceded by the preposition उप is <i>Atmanepadi</i> when it means 'to espouse' by Rule (1. 3. 56).
