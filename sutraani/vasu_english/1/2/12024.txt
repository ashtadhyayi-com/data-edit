After the above three verbs, the <i>ktva</i> affix being <i>set</i>, may or may not be <i>kit</i>. As वचित्वा or वंचित्वा 'having cheated.' So also लुचित्वा or लुंचित्वा 'having plucked', ऋतित्वा or अर्तित्वा 'having dared.'

When the verb ऋत् <i>rit</i> takes the affix ईयङ् it is not governed by this <i>sutra</i>. See <i>sutra</i> [[3.1.29]].
1.2.25.md
When the <i>ktva</i> is not <i>set</i>, but <i>anit</i>, then there is no option allowed. As वच् + क्त्वा = वक्त्वा.
