The phrase <i>purvasya</i> <i>karta</i> of the last is to be read into this aphorism. As होत्रे ऽनुगृणाति. They encourage the <i>Hotri</i>, i.e. the <i>Hotri</i> priest invokes first, the others then follow him in invocation and by so doing encourage him. The word अनुगरः and प्रतिगरः mean encouraging the invoker.
