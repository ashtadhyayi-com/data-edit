After the Desideratives of श्रु 'to hear', when preceded by प्रत and आङ् , the आत्मनेपद affix is Not used.
The verb <i>sru</i> when taking the prefixes <i>prati</i> and <i>an</i>, is not <i>atmanepadi</i>, though taking the desiderative "<i>san</i>." This is also an exception to the rule of <i>sutra</i> [[1.3.57]]. Thus प्रतिशुश्रूषति and आशुश्रूषति ॥

The word <i>prati</i> and <i>an</i> must be <i>upasargas</i>; if they are used as <i>karma</i> <i>pravachaniyas</i> (see (1.4.—83)) then the rule will not apply, as देवदत्तं प्रतिशुश्रूषते ॥
