After the verb रम् 'to sport', preceded by वि and आङ् , परस्मैपद is used.
The verb रम् means 'to sport.' It is <i>anudattet</i> and therefore by <i>sutra</i> [[1.3.12]] it would have taken <i>Atmanepada</i> terminations; the present <i>sutra</i> ordains <i>parasmaipada</i> instead, as विरमति 'he takes rest'; आरमति 'he delights in,' परिरमति 'he sports.'
