The affixes of the परस्मैपद are used optionally after the denominative verbs ending in the affix क्यष।
The affix क्यष is ordained by <i>sutra</i> [[3.1.12]] भृशादिभ्यो भूव्यच् वेर्लोपश्च हलः after the words लोहित &c. These roots take optionally <i>parasmaipada</i>. As लोहितायति or ते he reddens. पटपटायति or ते he makes <i>pat</i> <i>pat</i>.
