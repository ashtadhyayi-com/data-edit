That which is especially auxiliary in the accomplishment of the action, of the verb दिव् 'to play' is called कर्म - object, as well as करण।
The present <i>sutra</i> ordains accusative case, where by the operation of the last aphorism there ought to have been Instrumental case. The force of च in the <i>sutra</i> is to indicate that <i>karana</i> is also to be read into this. As अक्षात् दीव्यति or अक्षै र्दीव्यति he plays the dice or with the dice.
