That which is the site of the verbs शीङ् 'to lie down', स्था 'to stand', आस् 'to sit', when preceded by the preposition अधि , is however called कर्म कारक or object.
This ordains accusative case, where otherwise by the last <i>sutra</i> there would have been the Locative case. As ग्राममधिशेते अधितिष्ठति or अध्यास्ते he lies down, occupies or lies in the village.
