The word तिरस् 'disappearance' is optionally called गति when the verb कृ follows.
This is an example of प्राप्त-विभाषा. As तिरः कृत्य or तिरस्कृत्य [[8.3.42]]: तिरः कृत्वा or तिरस्कृत्वा [[8.3.42]]. The change of <i>visarga</i> into स is optional [[8.3.42]]. When not meaning disappearance we have तिरः कृत्वा काष्ठं तिष्ठति he stands having laid aside the stick.
