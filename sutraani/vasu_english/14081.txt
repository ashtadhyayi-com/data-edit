In the छन्दस् (Veda) these गति and उपसर्ग are employed indifferently after the verbal root, as well as before it.
As.-- वायविन्द्रश्च सुन्वत आयातमुपनिष्कृतम् ॥ मक्ष्वित्था धिया नरा (<i>Rig</i>.I.2.6).

In this we have आयातमुप instead of उपायतम् '<i>vayu</i> and thou <i>Indra</i>, ye heroes, come ye both quickly to the <i>soma</i> of the worshipper by this sincere prayer.'
