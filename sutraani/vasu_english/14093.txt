The words अधि and परि are कर्म-प्रवचनीय when used as mere expletives.
As कुतोऽभ्यागच्छति whence has he come? Or कुतः पर्या गच्छति. These words though mere surplusages, have been called <i>karmapravachaniya</i> so that they may not get the designation of <i>upasarga</i> or <i>gati</i> and thus produce results peculiar to those words.
