A numeral (संख्या) may be compounded with the names of 'rivers' and the resulting compound is अव्ययीभाव denoting an aggregate.
According to <i>Patanjali</i> this refers to their Aggregate (समाहार); as सप्तगङ्गम् ' at the meeting of the seven <i>Ganges</i>,' द्वियमुनं 'at the meeting of the two <i>Yamunas</i>,' पञ्चनदं' where the five rivers meet'. सप्तगोदावरम् 'where seven <i>Godavaris</i>' meet.
