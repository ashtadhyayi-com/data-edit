And the words मयूर-व्यंसका 'cunning like a peacock' etc. are तत्-पुरुष compounds.
These are irregularly formed <i>Tat-purusha</i> compounds. The force of the word च in the aphorism is that of restriction. For though the compound like मयूरव्यंसक is allowable, we cannot form a compound like परम मयूरव्यंसक.
