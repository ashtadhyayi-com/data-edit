The उपसर्जन [[1.2.43]] is to be placed first in a compound.
The word समासे is understood here. The <i>upasarjana</i> being the word exhibited in the nominative case in the rules relating to <i>samasa</i>, must stand first. The constant application of this rule has been illustrated in the previous aphorisms. Without this rule, there would have been no fixity as to the position of words.
