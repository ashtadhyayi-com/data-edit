The words कडाराः and the like, are optionally placed first in the कर्मधार्य।
Thus कडारजैमिनिः or जैमिनिकडारः <i>Kadarajaiminih</i> or <i>jaiminikadarah</i>. 'The tawny Jaimini.'

1.कडार. 2. गडुल. 3. खञ्ज. 4.खोड. 5.काण. 6.कुण्ठ. 7.खलति . 8. गौर. 9.वृद्ध.  10. भिक्षुक. 11. पिङ्ग. 12. पिङ्गल. 13. ( पिङ्गल). 14. तद्ध. 15.तनु 16. जठर. 17. बधिर. 18. मठर. 19.कञ्ज. 20. बर्बर.

This <i>sūtra</i> enjoins an option where by general rule guna words being attributes would have invariably stood first.

Why do we say 'in the <i>karmadharaya compound'? Observe कडारपुरुषो ग्रामः 'a village of tawny men,' which is </i>Bahuvreehi_. Here ends the force of [[1.4.1]] and [[2.1.3]].
