The 5th case-affix (Ablative) is used when a word is governed by the following कर्मप्रवचनीयाः [[1.4.83]] i.e. अप , आङ् and परि।
Ex. अप or आ or परि पाटलिपुत्रादावृष्टो देवः 'It rained off or upto or with the exclusion of, <i>Pataliputra</i>.'

The परि here has the meaning of 'exclusion' [[1.4.88]] being read along with अप, therefore, not here वृक्षं परि विद्योतते विद्युत् [[1.4.90]].
