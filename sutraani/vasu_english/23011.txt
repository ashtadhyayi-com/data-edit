The 5th case-affix (Ablative) is used after whatsoever is governed by a कर्मप्रवचनीयाः [[1.4.83]] in the sense of 'substitute' or 'exchange' -- प्रतिः प्रतिनिधिप्रतिदानयोः [[1.4.92]].
Ex. अभिमन्युरर्जुनतः प्रति '<i>Abhimanyu</i> is the representative of <i>Arjuna</i>.' माषानस्मैतिलेभ्यः प्रतियच्छति 'he exchanges <i>mashas</i> for this sesamum.' See [[1.4.92]] for an explanation of प्रतिनिधि and प्रतिदान.
