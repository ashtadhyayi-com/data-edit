By whatsoever limb, being defective, is pointed out the defect of the person after that the 3rd case-affix is used.
As, अक्ष्णा काणः 'blind of one eye' पादेन खंजः 'lame of foot.' पाणिना कुण्ठः &c. The word <i>anga</i> in this <i>sutra</i> applies to the whole body, whatsoever by reason of being a member of the body is defective is indicated here.
