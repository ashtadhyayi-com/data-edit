And when लुङ् (Aorist) follows वध is the substitute of हन्।
अवधीत् 'he killed,' अवधीष्टाम् 'they two killed.' The division of this aphorism from the last <i>sutra</i> (<i>yoga</i>-<i>vibhaga</i>) indicates that the next rule applies to Aorist only and not to Benedictive as well. There is no option in Benedictive <i>Atmanepada</i>.
