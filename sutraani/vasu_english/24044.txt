When आत्मनेपद affixes follow, वध् is optionally the substitute of हन् in the लुङ् (Aorist).
Thus आवधिष्ट or आहत; आवधिषाताम् or आहसाताम्; आवधिशत or आहसत.

The verb हन् becomes <i>Atmanepadi</i> when preceded by आ [[1.3.28]] By <i>sutra</i> [[1.1.14]], the Aorist-affixes in the <i>Atmanepada</i> are कित् and हन् and by [[6.4.37]] the न is elided before कित् affixes then by [[8.2.27]] the स is elided. Thus आहन् + सिच् + त = आहत.
