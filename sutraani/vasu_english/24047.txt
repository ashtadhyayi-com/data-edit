And when सन् (Desiderative) follows, गमि is the substitute of ण् when the sense is not that of 'informing'.
जिगमिषति 'wishes to go' जिगमिषतः, जिगमिषन्ति. But अर्थान् प्रतीषिषति 'he wishes to inform the meaning.' So also with इक्, as अधिजिगमिषति. The <i>yogavibhaga</i> indicates that the <i>anuvritti</i> of सन् only runs in the next <i>sutra</i> and not of णि. The form is thus evolved in the <i>Parasmaipada</i>; सन् + गम् = गम् + गम् + स [[6.1.9]] = ग + गम् + स [[7.4.60]] = ज + गम् + स [[7.4.62]] = ज + गम् + इट् + स [[7.2.50]] = जिगमिष, and then we add the personal terminations.
