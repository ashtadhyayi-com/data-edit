After the verb अद् 'to eat' and the rest there is लुक् elision of the विकरण शप् -- कर्तरि शप् [[3.1.68]].
Thus अद् + शप् + ति = अत्ति 'he eats'; हन्ति 'he kills;' द्वेष्टि 'he envies.' The <i>Adadi</i> verbs belong to the second conjugation. In this class of verbs, therefore, the terminations are added direct to the root.
