In the छन्दस् (Vedas) there is diversely the लुक् elision of the विकरण शप् -- कर्तरि शप् [[3.1.68]].
There is elision in other conjugations than <i>Adadi</i>; and there is sometimes even no elision in <i>Adadi</i> verbs. As वृत्रं हनति 'he kills <i>Vritra</i>.' हन् + शप् + ति = हनति instead of हन्ति । So also, अहिः शयते instead of शेते. (<i>Rig</i>.V. I.32.5). In these cases the <i>Vikarana</i> शप् has not been elided.

In some cases the शप् is elided as त्राध्वम् नो देवाः instead of त्रायध्वम् ॥
