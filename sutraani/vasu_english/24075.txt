After the verbs हू 'to sacrifice' and others there is श्लु elision of the विकरण शप् -- कर्तरि शप् [[3.1.68]].
These verbs belong to the <i>Juhotyadi</i> class. जुहोति 'he sacrifices' [[7.1.10]]. श्लु causes the reduplication of the root. So also बिभेति 'he fears.' निनेक्ति from निज् 'to purify, he purifies.'
