डा , रौ and रस् are substituted respectively in the room of the three-affixes of the 3rd Person of लुट् (First Future), both in the परस्मैपद and आत्मनेपद। 
Thus in the <i>Parasmaipada</i> कर्ता 'he will do,' कर्तारौ 'they two will do;' कर्तारः 'they will do.' So also in the <i>Atmanepada</i> we have अध्येता, अध्येतारौ, अध्येतारः ।

Why do we say 'in the 3rd person'? Observe श्वः कर्तासि । श्वोऽध्येतासे ॥
