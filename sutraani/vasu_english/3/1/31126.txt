The verb आसु is a compound verb formed by the preposition आङ् and the root सु. This <i>sutra</i> debars यत् that would otherwise have come by <i>sutra</i> 97 and 98.

Thus आसु + ण्यत् = आसाव्यम्, (VII. 2. II5) and (VI. I. 79); so also याव्यम्, वाप्यम्, राप्यम्, लाप्यम्, त्राप्यम् and आचाम्यम्.

The force of the word च 'and' in the <i>sutra</i> is to indicate that the roots not included in the above enumeration should also be taken. Thus दभ् + ण्यत् = दाभ्यम् ॥
