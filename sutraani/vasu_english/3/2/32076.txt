And the affix क्विप् is also seen after all verbs, whether having an उपपद or not in the Vedic as well as in the modern Sanskrit.
As उखायाः स्रंसते = उखास्रत् [[6.4.24]] 'falling from the pot'; पर्णध्वत् 'falling from the leaves'; वाहाद् भ्रश्यति = वाहाभ्रश्,1st. sing. वाहाभ्रट् 'falling from a car'. The हा is long by [[6.3.137]]. See <i>Rig</i>. <i>Ved</i>. I. 3. 8).
