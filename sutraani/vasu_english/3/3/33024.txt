This debars the affix अच् [[3.3.56]]. As श्रायः 'shelter', नायः 'a means', भावः 'a condition'.

But with <i>upasargas</i>, we have प्रश्रयः 'respect', प्रणयः 'love'; प्रभवः 'source'.

How do we explain the form प्रभाव in प्रभावो राज्ञः 'the splendour of the king'? Here the word प्रभाव is not made from the compound root प्रभू + घञ्, but by compounding प्र with भाव by the rules of <i>samasa</i>, meaning 'excellent condition'.

How do you explain the form नय in नयो राज्ञः ? Here the root नी has taken the affix अच् diversely by [[3.3.113]].
