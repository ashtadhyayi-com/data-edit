The case-affix es (सुप्) and the affixes having an indicatory प (पित्) are अनुदात्त।
This is an exception (<i>apavada</i>) to the last aphorism. As दृसदौ 'two stones'; the case-affix औ is <i>anudatta</i>; so also तृपदः; so also पचति; The third person singular affix तिप् has an indicatory प, and is <i>anudatta</i>. But not पचतः.
