The affix अण् comes after a root हूञ् 'to call' वेञ् 'to weave', माङ् 'to measure', when the object is in composition with it.
This debars the affix  क [[3.2.3]]; as स्वर्गे + ह्वे + अण् = स्वर्गे + ह्वा + अ = स्वर्गे + ह्वाय + अ [[7.3.33]] = स्वर्गह्वायः (the य is added by rule [[7.3.33]], 'when a <i>krit</i> affix with an indicatory ण or म follows', the augment य is added after the verbs that end in long आ). So also तन्तुवायः, धान्यमायः ।
