And the affix खच् comes after the verb गम् 'to go' when in composition with a word ending in a case-affix and when the word to be formed denotes a name.
As सुतङ्गमः '<i>Sutangama</i>'. Though the verb गम् could well have been included in the last aphorism, the separation serves the purpose of carrying the <i>anuvritti</i> of गम् into the subsequent <i>sutras</i>.
