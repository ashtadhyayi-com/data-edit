The affix णिव after the verb वह् 'to carry' in the छन्दस् (Vedas), when the word ending in case-affix is in composition with it.
As प्रष्ठवाह् Ist sing. प्रष्ठवाट् 'carrying a <i>prashtha</i> measure'; so, दित्यवाट् ।

This division of this <i>sutra</i> from the last is for the sake of the subsequent <i>sutras</i>, into which the <i>anuvritti</i> of वह् only is carried and not of सह्. See <i>Yajur</i>. <i>Veda</i>. (XIV. 10), (XVIII.26).
