The affix णिनि comes after the verb यज् 'to sacrifice' with the sense of past time, when the word in composition is the Instrumental -3rd case.
The <i>anuvritti</i> of णिनि and not of खश् is to be read into this <i>sutra</i>. As अग्निष्टोमयाजिन् 'who has sacrificed with <i>Agnishtoma</i>. Here <i>Agnishtoma</i> is the instrument for the attainment of the desired fruit.
