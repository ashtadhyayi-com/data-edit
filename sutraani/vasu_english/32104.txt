The affix अतृन् comes after the verb जृ 'to grow old' with the sense of past time.
As जृ + अतृन् = जर् + अव् [[7.3.84]] = जरत्, 1st. sing. जरन्, 1st. dl. जरन्तौ, 1st. pl. जरन्तः 'grown old' [[6.1.7]].

By the rule of वासरूप [[3.1.94]], the <i>Nishtha</i> affixes also come after this verb. As जीर्णः and जीर्णवान् । जृ + क्त = जिर् + त [[7.1.100]] and [[1.1.51]] = जिर् + न [[8.2.42]] = जीर्ण [[8.2.77]] and [[8.4.1]] 'grown old'.
