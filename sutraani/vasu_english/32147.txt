The affix वुञ् comes after the verbs देवि 'to lament' and क्रुश 'to cry' when an उपसर्ग is in composition with them.
As आदेवकः 'one who sports or laments'; आक्रोशकः 'vociferous, a reviler'; परिदेवकः 'one who laments'; परिक्रोशकः 'a reviler'.

Why do we say 'when in composition with an <i>upasarga</i>'? Observe देवयिता and क्रोष्ठा formed by तृन् [[3.2.135]].
