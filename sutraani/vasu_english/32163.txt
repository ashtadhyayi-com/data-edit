The affix क्वरप् comes in the sense of 'agent having such a habit etc.' after the verbs 1. इ 'to go' 2. नश् 'to destroy' 3. जि 'to conquer' 4. सृ 'to flow'.
The प् is indicatory in order to introduce the augment तुक् between the verb and the affix [[6.1.71]]. Thus इ + क्वरप् = इ + त् + वर = इत्वरः, fem. इत्वरी 'a traveller'; नश्वरः, fem. नश्वरी 'transitory'; जित्वरः, fem. जित्वरी 'victorious'; सृत्वरः fem. सृत्वरी 'going, a river'.

Though the affix begins with a letter of वल् <i>pratyahara</i>, and ought to have taken the intermediate इट् [[7.2.35]] yet it is prohibited by [[7.2.8]].
