The affix घञ् comes after the verb स्तु 'to praise' when it is preceded by the उपसर्ग सम् and the word refers to sacrificial subjects.
As संस्तावश्छन्दोगानाम् 'the <i>Sanstava</i> of the <i>Chhandogas</i>'; संस्ताव (= समेत्य स्तुवन्ति यस्मिन् देशे छन्दोगाः स देशः) means 'the place which the <i>Brahmanas</i> repeating hymns and prayers, occupy at a sacrifice'.

But when not referring to sacrifice we have संस्तवश्छात्रयोः 'the praising together of the two pupils'.
