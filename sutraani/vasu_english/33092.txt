The affix कि comes under similar circumstances after a घु verb -- दाधा घ्वदाप् [[1.1.20]] when an उपसर्ग is in composition with it.
The क् is indicatory, by force of which the final आ is elided [[6.4.64]]. Thus प्र + दा + कि = प्रदिः 'a present'; प्रधिः 'the periphery of a wheel'; उपाधिः 'fraud'; अन्तर्द्धिः 'vanishing'.
