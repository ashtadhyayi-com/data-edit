The affix अण् comes after those verbs which end in long आ when an उपसर्ग is in composition and when the word to be formed is feminine.
This supersedes क्तिन्. Thus प्रदा, प्रधा, उपदा, उपधा, &c.

Vart:- For the purposes of this rule the particles श्रत् and अन्तर् should be treated like <i>upasargas</i>. Thus श्रद्धा 'faith' अन्तर्द्धा [[1.4.59]].
