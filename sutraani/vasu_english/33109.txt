The affix ण्वुल् comes after the verbal roots, when the word to be formed is an appellative.
As उद्दालकपुष्पभञ्जिका 'a sort of game played by the people in the eastern districts in which <i>uddalaka</i> flowers are broken or crushed'. वरणपुष्पप्रवाहिका 'a sort of game'; आभ्यूषखादिका; आचोषखादिका; शालभञ्जिका; तालभञ्जिका ।
