The word स्म is understood here. The word अधीष्ट has already been explained in <i>sutra</i> [[3.3.161]]. This rule supersedes the Potential. Thus अङ्ग स्म राजन् माणवकमध्यापय or अङ्ग स्म राजन्नग्निहोत्रं जुहुधि 'O! King! I wish that you teach the boy or make <i>Agnihotra</i> sacrifice'.
