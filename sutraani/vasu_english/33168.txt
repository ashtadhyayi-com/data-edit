The affix लिङ् (Benedictive) comes after a root when the word यद् is in composition and the words 1. काल 2. समय 3. वेला occur in construction.
This ordains the Potential and supersedes the Infinitive in '<i>tumun</i>'. Thus कालः, समयः, वेला वा यद् भवान् भुञ्जीत 'it is time that your honor should take your meals'.
