The affix लेट् (Vedic Subjunctive) is optionally used the छन्दस् (Vedas) wherever the लिङ् (Benedictive) can be used.
Thus in the sense of 'command' &c., he may be employed instead of लिङ्. This is called the Vedic Subjunctive and is peculiar to the Vedas only. Thus जोषिषत् (<i>Rig</i>. II.35.1) 'may he make us beautiful'. तारिषत् (<i>Rig</i>. I.25.12) 'may he increase'. पताति विद्युत् (<i>Rig</i>. VII.25.1) 'may the thunder-bolt fall'. भवाति (<i>Rig</i>. V. 37.5) 'may he become'. So also मन्दिषत्, नेता, नेषत्, तक्षिषत्, उदधिं च्यावयाति ।
