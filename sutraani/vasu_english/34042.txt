The affix णमुल् comes after the बन्ध् 'to bind' when a word so formed denotes an appellative.
Thus क्रौञ्चबन्धं बध्नाति 'he binds in the manner called <i>krauncha</i>-<i>bandhan</i> or 'heron-knot'. मयूरिकाबन्धं बध्नाति or बद्धः 'he binds or is bound in a 'peacock-knot'. अट्टालिकाबन्धं बद्धः 'bound in a knot called <i>attalika</i>-<i>bandhan</i>'.

All the above, <i>krauncha</i>-<i>bandhan</i> &c. are names of various sorts of 'bonds or knots'. The rule [[3.4.46]] also applies here.
