The words भीम etc. are irregularly formed and denote ablation.
These words are formed by <i>Unadi</i> affixes. Thus भी + मक् = भीम (<i>Un</i>. I.145.148); भी + षुक् + मक् =भीष्म (<i>Un</i>. I.148) &c. By the next <i>sutra</i>, <i>Unadi</i> words do not denote ordinarily the recipient and the ablation.

The present <i>sutra</i> makes them do so in the case of भीमादि words. The following is a list of <i>Bhimadi</i>words :- भीमः, भीष्मः, भयानकः (<i>Un</i>. III.82), वरुः, चरुः, (<i>Un</i>. I.7), भूमिः (<i>Un</i>. IV. 45), रजः (<i>Un</i>. IV. 217), संस्कारः, सङ्क्रन्दनः, प्रपतनः, समुद्रः, स्रुचः, or स्रुवः (<i>Un</i>. II.61),स्रक् (<i>Un</i>. II.62) खलतिः ।
