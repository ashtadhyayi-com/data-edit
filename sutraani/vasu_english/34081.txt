The words एश् and इरेच् are the substitutes of ट and झ respectively in the लिट् (Perfect Tense).
The श् of एश् indicates that it replaces the whole of the affix त [[1.1.55]]. The च् of इरेच् is for the sake of accent [[6.1.165]]. Thus पेचे, पेचाते पेचिरे । See the foregoing table of tenses for the <i>Atmanepada</i>.
