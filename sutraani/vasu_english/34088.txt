In the छन्दस् (Vedas) the substitute of हि is optionally treated as not having an indicatory प्।
The result is that in the <i>Chhandas</i> the second person singular of लोट् has two forms as प्रीणाहि or प्रीणीहि; युयुधि or युयोधि, धि being substituted for हि in the Vedas, by rule [[6.4.103]] as in the following verse:

अग्ने॒ नय॑ सु॒पथा॑रा॒येऽअ॒स्मान्विश्वा॑निदेव व॒यूना॑निविद्वा॒न् ।
यु योध्य॒स्मज्जु॑हुरा॒णमेनो॒भूयि॑ष्ठान्ते॒ नमाऽउक्तिम्विधेम ॥

                          --<i>Rig</i> 1.189. 1: <i>Yajur</i> <i>Veda</i>. 40.16.
