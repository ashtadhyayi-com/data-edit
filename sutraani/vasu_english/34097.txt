In the परस्मैपद affixes the इ is optionally elided in the लेट् (Vedic Subjunctive).
The वा of the last <i>sutra</i> is understood here also. Thus जोषिषत् (<i>Rig</i>. II.35. I). तारिषत् (<i>Rig</i>. I.25.12. मन्दिषत् । In the alternative, it is not elided as पताति विद्युत् (<i>Rig</i>. VII.25.1). उदधिं च्यावयाति । The इ of इट्, वहि and महि of the <i>Atmanepada</i> are not elided.
