The फक् of this <i>sutra</i> has, like that of the preceding <i>sutra</i>, the force of a <i>Yuvan</i> affix, since it is to be added to words formed by the <i>Gotra</i> affixes यञ् and इञ् ॥ Thus गर्ग + यञ् (IV. I. 105) = गार्ग्यः ॥ गार्ग्य + फक् = गार्ग्यायणः ॥ दक्ष + इञ् = दाक्षिः; दाक्षि + फक् = दाक्षायणः ॥ It is not every word which ends in यञ् or इञ् which takes the <i>Yuvan</i> affix फक्; but only that word where the affix यञ् or इञ् has the force of a <i>Gotra</i> affix. Thus the affix यञ् is added by [[4.3.10]] to denote proximity to a sea. As द्वीप + यञ् = द्वैप्यः ॥ This word will not take the affix फक् ॥ Similarly by [[4.2.80]] the affix इञ् is added to the words सुतंगम &c, the force of the affix being चातुरर्थिक ॥ Thus सौतंगमिः. This word will not take the affix फक् ॥
