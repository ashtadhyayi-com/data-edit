Thus वातण्ड्यः = आंगिरसः, otherwise वातण्डः ॥

The word वतण्ड occurs both in the <i>Gargadi</i> class [[4.1.105]] and in the <i>Sivadi</i> class [[4.1.112]]. It would have taken both affixes अञ् and अण्. The present <i>sutra</i> declares that when it means an <i>Angirasa</i>, then वतण्ड will not take अण् of the शिवादि class : and when it does not mean <i>Angirasa</i>, then it takes both these affixes e. g. वातंड्यः and वातंडः any descendant of <i>Vatanda</i>.
