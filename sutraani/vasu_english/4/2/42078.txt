This debars अञ् of [[4.2.74]]. This <i>sutra</i> consists of one word रोणी in the nominative case. As a general rule all words to which affixes are to be added are put in the ablative case in these <i>sutras</i>. The fact of this exceptional treatment of the word रोणी indicates that this word takes अण् under all circumstances, whether alone, or forming part of a compound. In fact, the nominative case in this <i>sutra</i> is a very ingenious explanatory artificiality. Thus रौणः, आजकरौणः or सैहिकरौणः
