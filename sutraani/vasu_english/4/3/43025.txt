The affixes अण् &c. घ &c. have been taught, but no particular sense was assigned to them. See [[4.2.92]]. This <i>sutra</i> gives one of the senses, and also declares the particular case in which the stem must be, to which the affix is to be added. Thus स्रुघ्नेजातः = स्रुघ्न + अण् = स्रौघ्नः 'born in <i>Srughna</i>'. So also माथुरः 'born in <i>Mathura</i>', औत्सः 'born in <i>Utsa</i>', औदपानः 'born in <i>Udapana</i>'. राष्ट्रियः [[4.2.93]], अवारपारीणः [[4.2.93]] शाकलिकः [[4.2.117]], ग्राम्यः [[4.2.94]], ग्रामीणः [[4.2.94]], कात्रेयकः [[4.2.95]] औम्भेयकः [[4.2.95]].
