Vart:- So also after बहिः, देव and पंचजन. As बाह्यम्, दैव्यम् and पांचजन्यम् ॥ The final of बहिस् is elided by the <i>vartika</i> under [[4.1.85]]. The word '<i>daivya</i>' could be also formed under another <i>Vartika</i> of the same.

Vart:- The affix <i>nya</i> comes after <i>chaturmasa</i> in the sense of a sacrifice; as चातुर्मास्यानि व्रतानि, चातुर्मास्यो यज्ञः, otherwise चातुर्मासः ॥
