The affixes इन , ख and य come in the sense of 'made by them' after the word पूर्व 'fore-fathers' in the Instrumental -3rd case in construction.
The ख is read into the <i>sutra</i> by force of the word च; the <i>anuvritti</i> of मत्वर्थ ceases. Thus पूर्वैः कृतं = पूर्विणः 'made by the ancestors' i. e. a road. So also पूर्व्यः and पूर्वीणः ॥ The word पूर्वैः in the plural means पूर्वपुरुषाः "past generations, ancestors". These words occur generally in the plural, and mean "roads widened by the forefathers". Thus गम्भीरेभिः पथिभिः पूर्विणेभिः ॥ So also, ये ते पन्थाः सविताः पूर्व्यासः (<i>Rig</i> I. 35. II) Another reading of this <i>sutra</i> is पूर्वैः कृतमिनियौच; the affixes then will be इनि (इन्), and य; and ख will be drawn in by virtue of च ॥ The examples then will be पूर्विन्; 3rd pl. पूर्विभिः (with इन्), as पथिभिः पूर्विभिः, or पूर्वीणैः (ख) or पूर्व्यैः (य) ॥
