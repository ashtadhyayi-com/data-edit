The affix तातिल् comes in the छन्दस् (Vedas) after the words 1. शिव 2. शम् and 3. अरिष्ट in the Genitive 6th-Case in construction when the sense is 'he does'.
The word करः is equivalent to करोति formed by अच् [[3.1.134]]

This shows that the construction must be genitive. With a <i>krit</i>-formed word, it has accusative force, as, शिवस्य करः = शिवं करोति ॥

Thus शिवस्य करः = शिवतातिः, So श॑तातिः and अरिष्टतातिः ॥ As याभिः॒॒ शन्ता॑ती॒॒ भव॑थो ददा॒॒शुषे॑ (<i>Rig</i> I.112.20) शंताती being dual of शन्तातिः, and meaning सुखस्य कर्तारौ; so also अथो अरिष्टतातये ॥
