The affix ङीष् does not come in the feminine after a प्रातिपदिक (Nominal Stem) denoting a part of the body when the word is subordinate in a compound, preceded by the word 1. सह 'with' 2. नञ् 'not' or 3. विद्यमान 'having'.
Thus सकेशा, अकेशा विद्यमानकेशा ॥ So also सनासिका अनासिका or विद्यमाननासिका ॥ The last example shows, that the present rule governs also [[4.1.55]] <i>ante</i>: as well as [[4.1.54]].
