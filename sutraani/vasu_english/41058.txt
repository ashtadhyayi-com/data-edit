The feminine affix ङीष् is not used after 1. नख 2. मुख when (the word at the end of which they stand) is a Name.
Thus शूर्प + नखा, = शूर्पणखा, '<i>Surpanakha</i> the sister of <i>Ravana</i>'. The न् is changed into ण् by [[8.4.3]] (पूर्वपदात् संज्ञायामगः) ॥ So also वज्रणखा, गौरमुखा, कालमुखा &c. Why do we say 'when denoting a Name'? Witness ताम्रमुखी कन्या 'a copper-faced damsel', or चन्द्रमुखी 'moon-faced'.
