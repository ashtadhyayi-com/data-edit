The feminine affix ऊङ् comes in denoting a Name after the words 1. कद्रु 'tawny' 2. कमण्डलु 'a water pot'.
This rule applies to other than Vedic forms. Thus कद्रूः 'a female named <i>Kadru</i> wife of <i>Kasyapa</i>', कमण्डलूः '<i>Kamandalu</i>'. Why do we say "in denoting a Name"? Observe कद्रूः 'the tawny color.' कमण्डलूः 'a water-gourd'.
