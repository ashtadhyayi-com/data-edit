The affix अण् comes after the प्रातिपदिक (Nominal Stem) अश्वपति etc., in the various mentioned senses antecedently up to तेन दीव्यति खनति जयति जितम् [[4.4.2]].
This is an exception to the next aphorism, by which the affix ण्य would have come after words ending with '<i>pati</i>.' The present <i>sutra</i> enjoins अण् instead. Thus अश्वपति + अण् = आश्वपतम् [[7.2.117]] तद्धितेष्वचामादे 'when a <i>Taddhita</i> affix follows with an indicatory ञ् or ण्, there is <i>vriddhi</i> in the room of the first rowel among the vowels'). The इ of पति is elided by [[6.4.148]] यस्येति च where long ई follows, and when a <i>Taddhita</i> affix follows, there is elision of the इ or ई, or अ or आ, of a <i>bha</i>'.)

The following is a list of words belonging to अश्वपत्यादि class :-

अक्षपति, 2 ज्ञानपति, 3 शतपति, 4 गणपति, 5 गणपति, 6 स्थानपति, यज्ञपति, 8 राष्ट्रपति, 9 कुलपति, 10 गृहपति, (पशुपति) 11 धान्यपति, 12 धन्वपति,13 बन्धुपति, 14 धर्मपति, 15 सभापति, 16 प्राणपति, 17 क्षेत्रपति ॥
