There is optionally लुक् elision of the युवन् affixes फक् and फिञ् when a प्राग्दीव्यतीय affix beginning with a vowel is to be added.
By the last <i>sutra</i>, all <i>Yuvan</i> affixes were told to be elided. The present <i>sutra</i> declares an option in the case of the <i>Yuvan</i> affixes फक् and फिञ्. Thus the patronymic of गर्ग is गार्ग्य [[4.1.105]]; the <i>Yuvan</i> of गार्ग्य is formed by फक् [[4.1.101]] गार्ग्यायणः ॥ The word denoting the pupils of <i>Gargyana</i> will be गार्गीय or गार्ग्यायणीयाः; वात्सीयाः or वात्सयायनीयाः ॥ Similarly of the affix फिञ् [[4.1.156]]. Thus from यस्क, the Patronymic is यास्क [[4.1.112]]. The <i>Yuvan</i> of the latter is यास्कायनि [[4.1.146]]. The pupils of <i>Yaskayani</i> will be called यास्कीया or यास्कायनीयाः ॥
