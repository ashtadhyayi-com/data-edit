The affix फक् comes in denoting a गोत्र (Patronymic) descendant after the words 1. शरद्वत् 2. शुनक 3. दर्भा when they denote the descendants of भृगु , वत्स and अग्रायण , respectively. 
Thus शारद्वातायनः = भार्गवः otherwise शारद्वतः (formed by अञ्) शौनकायनः = वात्स्यः, otherwise शौनकः, दार्भायणः = आग्रायणः, otherwise दार्भिः, (formed by इञ् [[4.1.95]]).

The words शरद्वत् and शुनक belonging to बिदादि class would by [[4.1.104]] have taken the affix अञ् in denoting <i>Gotra</i>, the present <i>sutra</i> prohibits that in certain cases and enjoins फक् instead.
