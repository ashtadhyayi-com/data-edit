The affix ढक् comes in the sense of a descendant after the stems शुभ्र etc.
This debars इञ् and other affixes. The word च in the <i>sutra</i> shows that some words not occuring in the list of शुभ्रादि may take the ढक्, as it is an आकृतिगणः ॥ For though the words गंगा, पांडव are not in the list, we find still forms like गांगेयः, पाण्डवेयः &c.

Thus शुभ्रस्यापत्यं = शौभ्रेयः ॥
