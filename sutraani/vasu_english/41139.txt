The affix ख comes in the sense of a descendant after the प्रातिपदिक (Nominal Stem) कुल and a compound word that ends in कुल।
In the next <i>sutra</i>, it will be taught that the word कुल when not preceded by any other word, takes the affixes '<i>yat</i>' and '<i>dhakan</i>.' That indicates by implication that the present <i>sutra</i> applies to the simple word कुल and to the words ending in कुल also. Thus कुलीनः, आढ्यकुलीनः, श्रोत्रियकुलीनः ॥
