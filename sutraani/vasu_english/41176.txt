In denoting a feminine name, the तद्राजा is elided after the words 1. अवन्ति 2. कुन्ति and 3. कुरु ।
That is to say the affix 'nyan'. [[4.1.171]] after the word '<i>Avanti</i>' and '<i>Kunti</i>', and the affix 'nya' after the word '<i>Kuru</i>' are elided when the word to be formed is the name of a female. Thus अवन्ती, कुन्ती and कुरु 'a daughter or princess of <i>Avanti</i>, <i>Kunti</i> of <i>Kuru</i>'. The word कुरु takes ऊङ् in the feminine by [[4.1.66]]. Why do we say 'in denoting the feminine'? Observe आवन्त्यः, कौरव्यः and कौन्त्यः ॥
