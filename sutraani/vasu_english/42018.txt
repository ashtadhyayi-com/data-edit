The affix ठक्
Thus दधनि संस्कृतं = दाधिकम् 'made of coagulated milk'. This form will be evolved by [[4.4.3]], but the दाधिकं formed by that rule means दध्ना संस्कृतं i. e. anything prepared or seasoned with curd; while the दाधिकं of this <i>sutra</i> means 'curd itself seasoned with salt or, pepper'.
