The affix अञ् comes after the words सुवास्तु etc. in the 4-fold sense.
This debars the affix अञ् which would have otherwise come by [[4.2.71]] and [[4.2.73]]. Thus सुवास्तोरदूरभवं नगरं = सौवास्तवम् ॥ So also वार्णवम् ॥ The use of the word अण्, though its <i>anuvritti</i> came from [[4.1.83]], is for the sake of indicating that this <i>sutra</i> debars <i>sutra</i> [[4.2.85]]. Thus सौवास्तवी नदी ॥
