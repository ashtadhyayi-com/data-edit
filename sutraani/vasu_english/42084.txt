The affixes ठक् and छ come after the word शर्करा in the 4-fold sense.
Thus of the word शर्करा there are altogether six forms denoting locality, namely two formed by this <i>sutra</i>, two by the last, and two by the <i>sutra</i> [[4.2.80]]. Thus शर्करा, शार्कर꣡म्, शर्करिक꣡म् (formed by ठच्), शार्करक꣡म् (formed by कक्), शार्करिक꣡म् (formed by ठक्), and शार्करीयम् (by छ).
