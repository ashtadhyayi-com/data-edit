The affix अञ् is added to the word मद्र , preceded by a word denoting 'direction', in the remaining senses.
Thus पौर्वमद्रः f. द्री; आपरमद्रः f. द्री ॥ Here contrary to the examples in the preceding <i>sutra</i>, the initial vowel is <i>vriddhied</i> by [[7.3.13]] which makes a special exception in the case of the word मद्र ॥
