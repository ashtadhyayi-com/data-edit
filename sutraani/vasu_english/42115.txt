The affixes ठक् and छस् come after वृद्ध word भवत् , in the remaining senses.
The word भवत् is a <i>Vriddha</i> word because it belongs to <i>tyadadi</i> class [[1.1.74]]. This rule debars छ of the last aphorism. In the affix, छस्, the final स् is indicatory, showing that the word भवत् before this affix gets the designation of <i>Pada</i> पद [[1.4.16]]. Thus भवत् + ठक् = भावत्कः;  भवत् + छस् = भवतीयः ॥

But when the word भवत् is not treated as <i>Vriddha</i>, then it takes the general affix अण्; as भावतः ॥ This is the case when भवत् is derived by affixing शतृ to भू ॥
