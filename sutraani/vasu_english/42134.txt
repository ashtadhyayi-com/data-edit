The affix वुञ् comes after the words कच्छ etc. in the senses of born etc. when the meaning is 'a man' or 'what exists in a man'.
This debars अण् ॥ Thus काच्छकः 'a man born in <i>Kutch</i>', काच्छकमस्य हसितं जल्पितम् ॥ 'His joke or talk is of <i>Kutch</i> or a <i>Kutch</i>-laughter'. काच्छिका चूडा ॥ Similarly सैन्धवको मनुष्यः, सैन्धवकमस्य हसितं जल्पितम्, सैन्धविका चूडा ॥

Why do we say "when meaning man or what exists in man?" Observe कान्छो गौः 'The cow of <i>Kutch</i>'. सैन्धवो वार्णवः ॥
