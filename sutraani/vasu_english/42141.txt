The affix छ comes in the remaining senses after a वृद्ध word denoting a place and ending with अक् or इक् or having ख as its penultimate.
The word देशे must be read into the <i>sutra</i>. This debars the अण् of <i>Sutra</i> 132 <i>ante</i>: as well as the affixes ordained by <i>Sutras</i> 117 and 123.

Thus अकः — आरोहणकीयम्, द्रौघणकीयम् ॥ इकः —आश्वपथिकीयम्, शाल्मलिकीयम् ॥ खोपधात् :-- कौटिशिखीयम्, आयोमुखीयम् ॥

Vart:- The words सौसुक &c. are also governed by this rule though they end in उक ॥ Thus सौसुकीयम्, मौसुकीयम्, ऐन्द्रवेणुकीयम् ॥
