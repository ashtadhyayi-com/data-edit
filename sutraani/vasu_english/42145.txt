The affix छ comes in the remaining senses after कृकण and पर्ण when denoting the country भारद्वाच।
The word देशे is understood here also. The word भारद्वाज does not here mean <i>Gotra</i>, but country. Thus कृकणीयम् and पर्णीयम् ॥

Why do we say "denoting country of <i>Bharadvaja</i>?" Observe कार्कणम्, पार्णम् &c.
