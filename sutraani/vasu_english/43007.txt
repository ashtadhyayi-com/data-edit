The affixes अञ् and ठञ् come in the remaining senses after the word अर्द्ध preceded by a word denoting direction when a particular portion of a village or an inhabited country.
The phrase दिकपूर्वपदात् is to be read into the <i>sutra</i>. This debars यत् ॥ Thus इमे खल्वस्माकं ग्रामस्य जनपदस्य वा पौर्वार्धाः or पौर्वार्धिकाः "Those verily belong to the eastern half of our village or country". So also दाक्षिणार्धाः or दाक्षिणार्धिकाः ॥ The word पौर्वार्द्धाः = ग्रामस्य पूर्वस्मिन्नर्द्धे भवाः and is a <i>Taddhitartha</i> compound.
