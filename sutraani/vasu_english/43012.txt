The affix ठञ् comes in the remaining senses after the word शरद् when expressing श्राद्ध।
This debars अण् [[4.3.16]]. The word श्राद्धे means the 'funeral oblation'; and not श्रद्धावान् पुरुषः 'a faithful or believing person'. Thus शारदिकं श्राद्धं 'the autumnal <i>Sraddha</i>'. When not meaning '<i>Sraddha</i>', it will be शारदम् ॥
