In the छन्दस् (Vedas), the affix ठञ् comes in the remaining senses after the word हेमन्त।
This debars अण् [[4.3.16]]. Thus सहश्च सहस्यश्च हैम꣡न्तिकावृतू ॥ The making of two separate <i>Sutras</i> of 20 and 21, is for the sake of the subsequent <i>sutra</i>, in which the <i>anuvritti</i> of word हेमन्त only is taken.
