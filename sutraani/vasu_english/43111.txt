The affix इनि comes in the sense of 'enounced by him' the thing enounced being भिक्षु-sūtra and नट-sūtra after the words 1. कर्मन्द 2. कृशाश्व।
Thus कर्मन्दिनो भिक्षवः, 'the mendicants who study the <i>Bhikshu</i>-<i>sutra</i> of <i>Karmandin</i>'. कृशाश्विनो नटाः 'the actors who study the <i>Nata</i> <i>Sutra</i> of <i>Krisasvin</i>.' Otherwise कार्मन्दकम् and काशीश्वम् ॥ See [[4.2.66]].
