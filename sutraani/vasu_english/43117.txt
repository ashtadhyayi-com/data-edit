An affix comes after a word in the Instrumental -3rd case in construction in the sense of 'made by him' when the whole word is a Name.
Thus मक्षिकाभिः कृतम् = माक्षिकम्, कार्मुकम्, सारघम्, पौक्तिकम् ॥ All these are names of 'honey', literally 'made by a bee', मक्षिका, सरघा and पुत्तिका meaning 'a bee'. मक्षिका + अण् = माक्षिक [[6.4.148]]. The <i>anuvritti</i> of "ग्रन्थ" does not, of course, apply here.
