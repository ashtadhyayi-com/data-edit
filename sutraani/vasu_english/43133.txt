The affix अण् comes in the sense of 'this is his' after the word आथर्वणिक and the penultimate इक् is elided.
This debars वुञ् [[4.3.126]]. Thus आथर्वणिकस्यायम् = आथर्वणो धर्म आम्नाथोवा "the <i>Atharvana</i> i. e. the Law or the Tradition of the <i>Atharvanikas</i>". The phrase चरणाद् धर्माम्नाययोः is understood here. This <i>sutra</i> is also a <i>vartika</i>.
