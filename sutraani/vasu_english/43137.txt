After a word having क् as its penultimate, the affix अण् comes in the sense of modification or a part or both, as appropriate.
This debars अञ्. Thus; तर्कु तार्क्कवम्, तित्तिडीक, तैत्तिडीकं; माण्डूकम्, दार्दुरूकम् माधूकम् ॥ The word तित्तिडीक &c have acute on the middle (<i>Phit</i> <i>Su</i>. II.16).
