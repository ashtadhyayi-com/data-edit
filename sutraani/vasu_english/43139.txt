The affix अण् comes in the sense of 'modification or part' (provided that the word has not the अनुदात्त - grave accent on the first syllable).
Thus देवदारवम्, भाद्रवारवम् ॥ The words देवदारु and भद्रदारु are <i>ady</i>-<i>udatta</i> owing to <i>Phit</i> <i>Su</i>. II.14 (पीतद्र्वर्थानाम् -- the trees denoting soft timber trees have acute on the first).
