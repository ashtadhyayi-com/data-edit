The affix अञ् comes in the sense of 'modification or part' after a word having अनुदात्त - grave accent on the first syllable.
This debars अण् ॥ Thus दाधित्थम्, कापित्थम्, माहित्थम् ॥ The word दधित्थम् is an <i>upapada</i>-<i>samasa</i> of दधि + स्थ, the स  being changed to त; and it is finally acute by कृदुत्तरपद प्रकृतिखर ॥
