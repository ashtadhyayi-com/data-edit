The affix अञ् comes optionally in the sense of 'modification or part' after the words पलाश etc.
Thus पालाशम् or पालाशम् so also खादिरम् or खादिरम्, यावासम् or यावासम् ॥

This <i>sutra</i> is an example of <i>prapta</i> and <i>aprapta</i> <i>vibhasha</i>. The words पलाश, खदिर, शिंशपा and स्यन्दन being gravely accented on the first syllable, the affix अञ् was obligatory by the last aphorism. This makes it optional, and is <i>prapta</i>-<i>vibhasha</i>. In the case of others it is <i>aprapta</i>-<i>vibhasha</i>.

1 पलाश, 2 खदिर, 3 शिंशपा, 4 स्यन्दन (स्पन्दन), 5 पूलाक, 6 करीर, 7 शिरीष, 8 यवास, 9 विकङ्कत.
