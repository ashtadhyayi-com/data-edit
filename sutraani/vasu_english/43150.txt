In the छन्दस् (Vedas) after a disyllabic word the affix मयट् comes in the sense of 'product or part'.
This ordains मयट् in the sacred literature also, secular literature being dealt with in [[4.3.143]]. Thus पर्णमयः, दर्भमयः, शरमयः in the following यस्य पर्णमयी जुहूर्भवति, दर्भमयम् वासो भवति, शरमयम् बर्हि र्भवति ॥
