The affix ठक् comes in the sense of 'who takes' after the words 1. प्रतिकण्ठा 2. अर्थ and 3. ललाम being in the Accusative -2nd case in construction.
Thus प्रतिकण्ठं गृह्णाति = प्रातिकण्ठिकः ॥ So also आर्थिकः, लालामिकः ॥ The word प्रतिकण्ठ here is an <i>Avyayi</i>-<i>bhava</i> meaning कण्ठं कण्ठं प्रति, and not प्रतिगतः कण्ठं = प्रतिकण्ठ, for to the latter no affix is added.
