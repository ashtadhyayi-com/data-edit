The affix यत् comes after words 1. मत 2. जन and 3. हल in the senses respectively of 1. 'means 2. 'gossip' and 3. 'drawing', the words being in the Genitive -6th-Case in construction.
Thus मतस्य करणं = म꣡त्यः 'the means of acquiring knowledge'. जनस्य जल्पः = ज꣡न्यः 'a rumour'. हलस्य कर्षः = ह꣡ल्यः 'ploughed'. So also द्विहल्यः, त्रिहल्यः, the <i>tadanta</i> <i>vidhi</i> applies रथसीताहलेभ्यो यद्विधौ ॥
