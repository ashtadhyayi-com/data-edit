The affix यत् comes in the sense of 'excellent in regard thereto' after a word in the Locative -7th case in construction.
Thus सामसु साधुः = सामन्यः॑ [[6.4.168]] 'conversant with the <i>Sama</i>-<i>Veda</i>'. So also वेमन्यः, कर्मण्यः, शरन्यः &c. The word साधु here means प्रवीण, or योग्य 'expert' and 'fit', and does not mean उपकारक 'a benefactor or a good person'. When the sense is that of 'good', the <i>sutra</i> तस्मै हितम् [[5.1.5]] will apply.
