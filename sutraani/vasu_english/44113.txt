The affixes ड्यत् and ड्य come in the sense of 'what stays there' after the word स्रोतस् and before these affixes the final syllable अस् of स्रोतस् is elided, in the छन्दस् (Vedas).
This debars यत् which comes in the alternative. As स्रोतसि भवः = स्रोत्यः॑ or स्रोत्यः॒꣡ the difference being in the accent [[3.1.3]] and [[6.1.185]]. The <i>anubandha</i> ड causes the elision of अस् of स्रोतस् ॥ When यत् is added the form is स्रोतस्यः॑ ॥
