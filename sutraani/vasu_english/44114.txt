The affix यन् comes in the sense of 'what stays there' after the words 1. सगर्भ 2. सयूथ and 3. सनुत।
This debars यत् the difference being in accent [[6.1.197]]. Thus अनुभ्राता सगर्भ्यः॑ 'a younger brother'. अनुसखासयू॑थ्यः 'a younger friend'. So also यो नः सनु॑त्यः उत वा जिघन्तु thief lit. 'who stays in a concealed place', <i>sanuta</i> meaning 'concealed' (see <i>Rig</i> <i>Veda</i> II.31.9). The word समान is always changed into स in the <i>Chhandas</i> [[6.3.84]].
