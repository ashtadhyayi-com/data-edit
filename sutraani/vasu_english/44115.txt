The affix धन् comes in the sense of 'what stays there' after the word तुग्र , in the छन्दस् (Vedas).
This debars यत् ॥ Thus तुग्रि॑यः as त्वमग्रे वृषभस् तुग्रियाणां ॥ Which assumes the form तुग्र्य <i>tugrya</i> also. As आवः शमम् वृषभं तुग्र्यासु  (<i>Rig</i> <i>Veda</i> l. 34.15) <i>Tugrya</i> meaning 'sunk in the waters'. The word तुग्र means 'food, firmament, sacrifice and <i>varishtha</i>'.
