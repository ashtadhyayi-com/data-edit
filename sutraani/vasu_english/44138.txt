The affix य comes in the छन्दस् (Vedas) after the word सोम with the force of मतुप्।
The force of the affix मयट् is that of ततः आगतः [[4.3.74]] and [[4.3.82]], विकारावयव [[4.3.134]] and [[4.3.143]] and प्रकृत [[5.4.21]]. The case in construction will vary according to the sense. Thus सोम्यं मधुः पिबन्ति = सोममयः मधुः ॥
