Thus आहतं रूपमस्य = रूप्यो दीनारः 'a <i>Dinara</i> having stamped on it the form of some king &c'. रूप्यः केदारः, रूप्यं कार्षापणम्. Similarly प्रशस्तं रूपमस्यास्ति = रूप्यः पुरुषः 'a lovely person'. The form impressed on a <i>dinar</i> &c by hammering &c, is called आहत or stamping.

Why do we say 'when stamping or praise is denoted ?' Observe रूपवान् 'having form'.

Vart:- The affix यप् is seen after other words also, as हिम्याः पर्वताः, गुण्या ब्राह्मणाः ॥
