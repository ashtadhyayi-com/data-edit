In the छन्दस् (Vedas), the affix थाल् comes in the sense of 'like this' after 1. प्रत्न 2. पूर्व 3. विश्व and 4. इम (इदम्).
Thus:-

तं प्र॒॒त्नथा॑ पूर्वथा॑ विश्वथेमथा॑ ज्येष्ठताति॑ बर्हिष॑दं स्व॒॒र्विदं ॥

(<i>Rig</i> <i>Veda</i> V. 44. 1). 'Him (<i>Indra</i>), as the ancients, as the predecessors, as all creatures, and as these living men have worshipped &c'.
