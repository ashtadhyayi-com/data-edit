The word दण्ड means "punishment", and व्यवसर्ग means "gift or donation". The <i>pratipadikas</i> पाद and शत preceded by a numeral, and conveying the sense of punishment or donation, take the affix वुन्, and the final अ is elided. This <i>sutra</i> is begun to show that the "distributive sense" does not apply here. Thus द्वौ पादौ दण्डितः = द्विपदिकाम् दाण्डितः "punished with the infliction of a fine of two <i>Padas</i>" (a quarter coin)". द्वौ पादौ व्यवसृजति = द्विपदिकां व्यवसृजात "he makes a gift of two <i>Padas</i>". Similarly द्विशतिकां दण्डितः "punished with a fine of two hundreds". द्विशतिकां व्यवसृजति &c.
