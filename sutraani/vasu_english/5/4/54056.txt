The <i>anuvritti</i> of <i>sati</i> ceases, that of <i>tra</i> continues. The verbs <i>kri</i>, <i>bhu</i> and <i>asti</i> also have no scope here. This is a general <i>sutra</i>. Thus देवात् गच्छति = देवत्रा गच्छति, देवेषु वसति = देवत्रा वसति ॥ So also मनुष्यत्रा, पुरुषत्रा, पुरुत्रा, and मर्त्यत्रा ॥

By using the word 'diversely' in the aphorism it is intended that the affix comes in this sense after other words also; as बहुत्रा जीवतो मनः ॥
