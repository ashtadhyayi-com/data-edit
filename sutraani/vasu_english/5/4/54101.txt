The words द्विगोः and अर्द्धात् are both understood here. Thus द्वे खार्यौ समाहृते = द्विखारम् or द्विखारि ॥ So also अर्धं खार्या = अर्धखारम् or अर्धखारी ॥ According to <i>Padamanjari</i> this <i>sutra</i> could be dispensed with: as खार is synonymous with, खारी, as द्विखारं, त्रिखारं ॥ And it will be govered by the <i>Vartika</i> ठन्येभ्योऽपि दृश्यते खार शताद्यर्थम् under [[5.1.57]] and [[5.1.58]], in <i>Mahabhashya</i>.
