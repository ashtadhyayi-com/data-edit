Thus शरदः समीपम् = उपशरदम्, प्रतिशरदम्, उपविपाशम्, प्रतिविपाशम् ॥

Why do we say when forming an <i>Avyayibhava</i> compound? Observe परमशरत् ॥ Those words of शरदादि class which end in a झय् consonant add invariably (नित्य) the affix <i>tach</i> in spite of [[5.4.111]]. The <i>anuvritti</i> of <i>avayayibhava</i> extends upto [[5.4.113]].
