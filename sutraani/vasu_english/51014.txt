The affix ञ्य (+/- य) comes, in the sense of a primitive serviceable for a product after the words ऋषभ and उपानह्, denoting the products.
Thus आर्षभ्यो वत्सः, औपानह्योः मुञ्जः ॥ This '<i>nya</i>' debars '<i>chha</i>'. Even when the primitive is 'leather चर्म', the affix '<i>nya</i>' will come after उपानह् irregularly debarring अञ् of [[5.1.15]]. Thus औपानह्यं चर्म ॥
