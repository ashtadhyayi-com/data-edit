Up to sutra [[5.1.115]] (excluding that) the affix ठञ् (+/- इक or +/- क) bears rule.
The affix ठञ् governs all the succeeding <i>sutras</i> upto तेन त्य्ल्यं क्रिय चेद्वात, and has the senses given to it in <i>Sutras</i> [[5.1.37]] &c. Strictly speaking the affix ठञ् governs only upto [[5.1.110]] and not upto [[5.1.115]]. Thus in <i>Sutra</i> [[5.1.72]], the affix ठञ् must be read as पारायणिकः, तौरायणिकः, चान्द्रायणिकः ॥
