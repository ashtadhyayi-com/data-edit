The affix कन् coming after a Numeral ending with वत्, takes optionally the augment इद् , the sense of the affix being those given up to [[5.1.63]]
The words ending in वत् are संख्या; see [[1.1.23]]: and would consequently take कन् by [[5.1.22]]. The present <i>sutra</i> ordains the optional affixing of इट् to this कन् ॥ Thus तावत्कः or तावतिकः, यावतिकः or यावत्कः ॥
