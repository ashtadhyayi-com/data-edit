The आर्हीय affix [[5.1.63]] is optionally elided after a द्विगु occasioning compound, of the word निष्क preceded by द्वि and त्रि।
The word द्विगोः 'a <i>taddhita</i> occasioning a <i>Dvigu</i> compound' of [[5.1.28]] is to be read into this <i>sutra</i>. Thus द्विनिष्कम् or द्विनैष्किकम्, त्रिनिष्कम् or त्रिनैष्किकम् [[7.3.17]].

Vart:- So also when <i>nishka</i> is preceded by बहु e.g. बहुनिष्कम् or बहुनैष्किकम् ॥ The irregular <i>Vriddhi</i> of the second term is caused by [[7.3.17]].
