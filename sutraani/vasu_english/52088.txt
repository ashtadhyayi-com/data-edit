The affix इनि (इन्) comes in the sense of 'by him' after इष्ट etc.
Thus इष्टमनेन = इष्टी यज्ञे; पूर्त्तीश्राद्धे &c. "who wished", "who performed a charitable act". The words इष्टिन्, पूर्त्तिन् &c. formed by इन् will govern locative instead of accusative. See <i>vartika</i> under [[2.3.36]].
