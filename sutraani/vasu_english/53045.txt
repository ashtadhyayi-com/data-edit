The substitute धमुञ् comes optionally in the place of धा after the words 1. द्वि and 2. त्रि।
Here also it comes in both the विधार्थ and अधिकरण विचाल senses. By च, the word 'optionally' is drawn into the <i>sutra</i>. Thus द्विधा or द्वैधम्, त्रिधा, or त्रैधम् ॥

Vart:- The affix ड (<i>da</i>, with the elision of the final vowel) comes without changing the sense, after words ending in <i>dhamun</i>. As द्वैध, त्रैध, in पथि द्वैधानि संश्रयन्ते, मति त्रैधानि संश्रयन्ते ॥
