The affixes इष्ठन् and इयसुन् come in the छन्दस् (Vedas) after a प्रातिपदिक (Nominal Stem) ending in तृ।
The तुः is ablative of तृ which includes both तृन् and तृच् ॥ This extends the application of these affixes to words other than attributes. Thus अतिशयेन कर्ता = करिष्ठः (कर्तृ + इष्ठन् = कर् + इष्ठ by [[6.4.154]], तृ is elided) As आसुर्ति करिष्ठः, दोहीयसी धेनुः ॥ By <i>Vartika</i> under [[6.3.35]] दोहित्री is changed into masculine दोहितृ and then ईयस् is added, when तृ is elided by [[6.4.154]].
