ज्य is also substituted for वृद्ध when the affixes इष्ठन् and इयसुन् follow.
Thus ज्येष्ठः 'the oldest', ज्यायान् 'the older'. By [[6.4.157]], वर्षि is also substituted for वृद्ध; and we have वर्षीष्ठः and वर्षीयान् also. The word वृद्ध though not a <i>gunavachana</i>, takes these affixes by the implication of this <i>sutra</i>.
