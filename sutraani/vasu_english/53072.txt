The letter द is the substitute of the final क् of an indeclinable when the affix अकच् (अक्) is added.
The word अव्यय is to be read into this <i>sutra</i> and not सर्वनाम, because no Pronominal ends in क् ॥ Thus धकित्, हिरकुत् and पृथकत् from धिक्, हिरुक् and पृथक् ॥ Thus हिरुक् + अक् = हिर् + अक् + उक् = हिरकुत्, now comes the present rule and the final क् is changed into द्, as हिरकुद् or त् ॥
