The affix ष्टरच् expresses slenderness after the words 1. वत्स 2. उक्षन् 3. अश्व and 4. ऋषव।
The <i>anuvritti</i> of हृस्व ceases. Thus वत्सतरः 'a weaned calf' becoming slender in reaching the second period of its age. उक्षतरः 'a slender bull': being the third age of a वत्स ॥ अश्वतरः 'a mule': (तस्य तनुत्वमन्यपितृकता); वृषभतरः 'a slender bull or that cannot draw the load'.
