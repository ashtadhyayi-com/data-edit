After a stem ending in the word देवता being in the Dative -4th Case in construction comes the affix यत् (यं) in the sense of 'appointed for it'.
The word तादर्थ्यम् has the same meaning as तदर्थ "for the sake of that" [[2.1.37]]. Thus अग्निदेवतायै इदम् = अग्निदेवत्यम् "offering &c, sacred to <i>Agni</i>". So also पितृदेवत्यम्, वायुदेवत्यम् ॥
