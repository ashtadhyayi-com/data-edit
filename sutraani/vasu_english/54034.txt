The affix ठक् comes without changing the sense after the word विनय etc.
Thus वैनयिकः = विनयः, सामयिकः ॥ The आ of उपाय is shortened, as, औपयिकः ॥ The word अकस्मात् should be read as अकस्माद् in order to apply इक् and not क, as, आकस्मिकः ॥ The टि is elided, because it is an <i>Avyaya</i>. See [[6.4.49]]. The affix is optional by [[4.1.82]].
