The affixes स and स्नौ come after मृद् when it means 'excellence'.
This debars the affix रूपप् [[5.3.66]]. Thus प्रशस्ता मृद् = मृत्सा or मृत्स्ना ॥ These two affixes are <i>nitya</i> or invariable, i. e. whenever superiority is to be expressed we must add these affixes. The reason for our calling it <i>nitya</i> is that the word अन्यतरस्याम् 'optionally' follows it in [[5.4.42]], so that this affix is not optional c. [[5.4.7]], [[5.4.10]], &c.
