The affix डाच् comes after the word 1. द्वितीय 2. तृतीय 3. शम्ब and 4. बीज when connected with the verb कृ 'to make' and meaning 'to plough in such a way'.
The repetition of the word कृ in this <i>sutra</i> indicates that the भू and अस्ति are not to be taken. Thus द्वितीया करोति = द्वितीयं कर्षे (= विलेखनं) करोति 'he makes the second ploughing of the field'. So also तृतीया करोति, शम्बा करोति 'he makes the field ploughed contrariwise to what it was ploughed before = अनुलोम कृषं क्षेत्रं पुनः प्रतिलोमं कृषति ॥ So also बाजी करोति = सहबीजेन विलेखनं करोति ॥

Why do we say when meaning "to plough" ? Observe द्वितीयं करोति पदम् ॥
