The affix डाच् comes after the word समय when कृ 'to make' follows, when the sense is that of 'neglecting'.
The word कृ is understood here also. The <i>anuvritti</i> of कृष ceases. The getting of leisure from the duties is called समय or 'time; the neglect or loss of such time is meant by the word यापना ॥ Thus समया करोति = कालक्षेपं करोति (अद्य मे पारवश्यं, श्वः परश्वो वास्य समय इत्येवं यो बहुषु दिवसेषु अतिवर्त्तते, स एवमुच्यते) 'who procrastinates'. Why do we say when the sense is that "neglecting"? Observe समयं करोति "he makes time".
