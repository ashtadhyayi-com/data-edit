The समासान्त affix does not come after a तत्-पुरुष समास of words like राजन् etc. herein taught, when the negative particle न precedes them.
Thus अराजा, असखा, अगौः [[5.4.91]] and [[5.4.92]] prohibited). Why do we say "after a <i>Tatpurusha</i> compound?" The same words preceded by the negative particle will take the <i>samasanta</i> affix when forming compounds other than <i>Tatpurusha</i>, as अणृचो मानवकः, अधुरं शकटम् ॥ [[5.4.74]] applied)).
