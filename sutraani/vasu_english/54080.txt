The affix अच् comes after the words 1. वसीयस् and 2. श्रेयस् when they follow the word श्वस् in a compound.
Thus श्वोवसीयसम्, श्वःश्रेयसम् ॥ These compounds belong to the class of <i>Mayuravyansakadi</i> [[2.1.72]] The word श्वः is usually employed in blessing or praising the object expressed by the second term. Thus श्वः श्रेयसम् ते भूयात् = शोभनं श्रेयस्ते भूयात् ॥ The word श्वोवसीयसम् is a synonym of श्वः श्रेयसम् ॥ The word वसीयः is derived from वसु meaning "praiseworthy", "rich", by adding the affix ईयसुन् [[5.3.57]].
