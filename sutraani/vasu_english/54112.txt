The affix टच् comes after an अव्ययीभाव समास ending in गिरि according to the opinion of the Grammarian सेनक।
Thus अन्तर्गिरम् or अन्तर्गिरि, उपगिरम् or उपगिरि ॥ The name of <i>Senaka</i> is mentioned for the sake of respect (<i>pujartha</i>), for the <i>anuvritti</i> of the word अन्यतरस्याम् "optionally" was understood here already.
