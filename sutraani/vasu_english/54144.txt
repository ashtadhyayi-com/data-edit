Optionally दत् is substituted for दन्त in a बहुव्रीहि समास when it is preceded by the words श्याव and अरोक।
Thus श्यावदन्तः or श्यावदन्, अरोकदन्तः or अरोकदन् ॥ अरोक means 'dark' 'non-luminous'. The word संज्ञायाम् of the last <i>sutra</i> governs this also, so that
when the compound is not a Name, the substitution does not take place; as श्यावदन्तः, अरोकदन्तः ॥
