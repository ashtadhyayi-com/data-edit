The बहुव्रीहि word त्रिककुद् formed by the elision of the final अ of ककुद is the name of a mountain.
त्रीणिककुदान्यस्य = त्रिककुत् "a three-peaked hill, the peaks having the shape of a hump". All three-peaked hills are not so called it is the name of a particular hill. When not denoting a hill we have त्रिककुदः ॥
