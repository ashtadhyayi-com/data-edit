Thus पटत्पटदिति or पटत्पटेति करोति (पटत्पटत् + इति = पटत्पट + इ + ति = पटत्पटेति) ॥ The word is doubled by [[8.1.4]]. The <i>para</i>-<i>rupa</i> substitution will take place when the whole word so reduplicated denotes a sound imitation, thus पटत्पटिति करोति ॥ Here we apply the preceding <i>sutra</i>. The <i>amredita</i> is the name of the second member of the doubled word [[8.1.2]].
