This applies when the forms are मम and तव, and not मे and ते ॥ Thus म꣡म स्वम्, त꣡व स्वम् ॥ The word युष्मद् and अस्मद् are derived from युष and अस by adding the affix मदिक् (<i>Un</i> I. 139) युष्मद् + ङस् = युष्मद् + अश् (VII. I. 27) = तव अट् + अश् [[6.2.96]] = तव꣡ + अ꣡श् [[7.2.90]] = तव (VI. I. 97). Here by [[8.2.5]], व would have been <i>udatta</i>, the present <i>sutra</i> makes त <i>udatta</i>. So also with मम ॥
