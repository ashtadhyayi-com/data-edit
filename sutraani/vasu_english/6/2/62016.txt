Thus ब्राह्मणसुखं पायसं "the sweet milk desired by or agreeable to the <i>Brahmanas</i>", छात्रप्रियोऽनध्यायः ॥ कन्याप्रियो मृदङ्गः ॥ &c. The words ब्राह्मण and छात्र have acute on the final being formed by the affixes अण् and ण [[5.4.62]] respectively (III. I. 3) and कन्या has <i>svarita</i> on the final. When not meaning agreeable to or desired, we have राजसुखम्, राजप्रियम् ॥
