Thus छा꣡त्रिशाला, ऐ꣡लिशाला, भा꣡ण्डिशाला ॥

Where the <i>Tatpurusha</i> compound ending in शाला becomes Neuter, by the option allowed in [[2.4.25]]; there also in the case of these words, the acute falls on the first syllable of the first term; thus superseding [[6.2.123]] which specifically applies to Neuter <i>Tatpurushas</i>. Thus छा꣡त्रिशालम्, ऐ꣡लिशालम् ॥

1 छात्रि, 2 पेलि (ऐलि), 3 भाण्डि, 4 व्याडि, 5 आखण्डि, 6 आटि, 7 गामि (गौमि) ॥
