A tense affix ending in अ is lengthened in the ऋग्वेद when the verb consists of two syllables.
Thus विद्मा हि त्वा सत्पतिं शूर गोनाम् &c. See <i>Rig</i> III. 42. 6, विद्मा हि तस्य पितरम् &c. But not here देवा भवत वाजिनः, as the verb consists of more than two syllables: not also here आ देवान् वक्षि यक्षि च as the verbs do not end in अ ॥
