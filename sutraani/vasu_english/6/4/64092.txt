The मित् roots are घटादि a subdivision of <i>Bhwadi</i> (800 to 873), and all other roots that end in अम् as शम् and तम् of <i>Divadi</i> class. Thus घटयति, व्यथयति, जनयति, रजयति, शमयति, ज्ञपयति ॥ Some read the <i>anuvritti</i> of the word 'optionally' from the last <i>sutra</i> into this. This will then be a limited option only (<i>vyavasthita</i>-<i>vibhasha</i>). The forms उत्क्रामयति and संक्रामयति are thus explained.
