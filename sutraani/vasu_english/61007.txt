In the room of a short vowel of the Reduplicate of the the roots तुज् etc., a long is substituted.
There is no list of तुजादि verbs given any where. The word आदि in तुजादि therefore should be construed as "verbs like <i>tuj</i>". So that wherever we may find a word having a long vowel in the Reduplicate, we should consider it a valid form. Thus तूतुजानः (तुज् + कानच् [[3.2.106]] = तूतुजानः <i>Rig</i>. I.3.6). मामहानः, अनड्वान् दाधार, स्वधां मीमाय, स तूताव ॥ This lengthening only takes place in the Vedas before some special affixes, and not every where or in secular literature. As तुतोज शबलान् हरीन् ॥
