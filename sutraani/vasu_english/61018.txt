Of the causative verb स्वपि 'to cause one to sleep', there is vocalisation of the semi-vowel, when the affix चङ् of the Reduplicated Aorist follows.
Thus the Aorist of स्वपि is असूषुपत्, असूषुपताम्, असूषुपन् ॥ The vocalisation takes place before reduplication, then there is <i>guna</i> of the penultimate short vowel, then this is again shortened by [[7.4.1]], then there is reduplication, and then lengthening of the vowel of the reduplicate by [[7.4.94]]. Thus स्वापि + चङ् = सुपि + चङ् = सुप् [[6.4.51]] + चङ् [[7.3.86]] = सोप् + चङ् = सुप् + चङ् [[7.4.1]] = सु + सुप् + चङ् [[6.1.11]] = सूषुपत् [[7.4.94]] which with the augment अ becomes असूषुपत् ॥  

Why do we say 'when चङ् follows'? Observe स्वाप्यते, स्वापितः ॥ The <i>anuvritti</i> of किति has ceased, that of ङिति however is here.
