The स्फी is the substitute of स्फाय 'to swell', the निष्ठा affixes follow.
Thus स्फीतः, स्फीतवान् ॥ Why do we say "when the <i>Nishtha</i> affixes follow"? Observe स्फातिः formed by क्तिन्  ॥ In the phrase स्फाती भवति, the word स्फाती is the feminine of the word स्फाति formed by क्तिन् ॥ The phrase निष्ठायाम् "when the <i>Nishtha</i> follows" governs all the subsequent <i>sutras</i> up to [[6.1.29]].
