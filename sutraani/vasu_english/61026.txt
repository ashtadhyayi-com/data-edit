There is optionally the vocalisation of the श्या followed by निष्ठा affixes, when the उपसर्गs अभि and अव precede it.
Thus अभिशीनम् or अभिश्यानम् घृतं, अवशीनम् or अवश्यानम् वृश्चिकः ॥ This option applies even when the word means "coagulation" and "cold". As अवशीनं or अवश्यानं घृतं, मेदः ॥ अवशीतो or अवश्यानो वायुः, &c. अवशीतं or अवश्यानमुदकं ॥

The पूर्व in this <i>sutra</i> serves the same purpose as in [[6.1.23]]. Thus अभिसंशीनं or अभिसंश्यानं, अवसंशीनं, अवसंश्यानं ॥ According to the author of <i>Siddhanta</i> <i>Kaumudi</i>, the word पूर्व shows that it is a <i>vyavasthita</i> <i>vibhasha</i>, hence there is no option allowed when अभि and अव are in the middle, as, समभिश्यानं, समवश्यानं ॥ Here we cannot have the alternative forms समभिशीनं &c.
