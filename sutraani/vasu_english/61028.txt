पी is optionally the substitute of the root प्याय् 'to increase' before the निष्ठा affixes.
The root ओप्यायी वृद्धौ belongs to <i>Bhvadi</i> class. The indicatory आ shows that the affixes त and तवत् are changed into न and नवत् [[8.2.45]]. Thus पीनं मुखम्, पीनौ बाहू, पीनसुरः ॥ The option here is also a regulated option (<i>vyavasthita</i>-<i>vibhasha</i>). The substitution takes place invariably when the root is without <i>upasarga</i>, and never when it is preceded by an <i>upasarga</i>. Thus प्रप्यानः आप्यानश्चन्द्रगाः ॥ The substitution, however, must take place when the preposition आङ् precedes the root, and the words अन्धुः and ऊधस् are in composition : as आपीनोन्धुः, आपीनमूधः ॥
