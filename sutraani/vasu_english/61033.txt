There is vocalisation of the semi-vowel of ह्वा in the reduplicated form, in both the syllables.
The <i>abhyasta</i> means the reduplicate and the reduplicated, both the syllables. The vocalisation takes place before reduplication. Thus जुहाव, जोहूयते, and जुहुषति ॥ This and the last <i>sutra</i> are one, in the original of <i>Panini</i>, they have been divided into two by the authority of a <i>Vartika</i>.
