The substitution of a long vowel homogenous with the first, does not take place when अ or आ is followed by a vowel (other than अ) of the case-affixes of the Nominative and the Accusative.
Thus वृक्षौ, प्लक्षौ, खट्वे, कुण्डे ॥ Here Rules [[6.1.87]] &c. apply. Why do we say "after अ or आ?" Observe अग्नी ॥ Why do we say "followed by an इच् (a vowel other than अ)"? Observe वृक्षाः here (VI.I.I02) applies.
