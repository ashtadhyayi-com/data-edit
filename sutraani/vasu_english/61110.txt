In the room of ए or ओ (in the body of a word), + अ of the case-affix अस् of the Ablative and Genitive Singular, the single substitute is the form of the precedent.
Thus अग्ने + अस् = अग्नेः, वायोः ॥ The इ and उ of अग्नि and वायु are gunated by [[7.3.111]]. This <i>sutra</i> applies when the ए or ओ are in the middle of a word and not <i>padanta</i>, as in the last. Thus अग्नेरागच्छति, वायोरागच्छति, अग्नेः स्वम्, वायोः स्वम् ॥
