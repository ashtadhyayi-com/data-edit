In the यजुर्वेद , the word उरस् when changed to उरो , retains it original form when followed by a short अ which is also retained.
Thus उरो अन्तरिक्षम् (<i>Yaj</i>. <i>Veda</i> <i>Vajasan</i>. IV. 7). Some read the <i>sutra</i> as यजुष्युरोः ॥ They take the word as उरू ending in उ, which in the Vocative case assumes the form उरो ॥ They give the following example उरो अन्तरिक्षं सजूर् ॥ But in the <i>Yajur</i> <i>Veda</i> VI.II, the text reads उरोरन्तरिक्षम् सजूः ॥ In the <i>Yajur</i> <i>Veda</i>, there being no stanzas, the condition of अन्तः पादं does not apply here.
