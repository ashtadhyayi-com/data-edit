In the यजुर्वेद , when the gravely accented अ of अवपथास् follows ए or ओ , the vowels retain their original form.
Thus त्री रुद्रेभ्यो अवपथाः ॥ The word अवपथाः is 2nd person Singular Imperfect of वप् in the <i>Atmanepada</i>. Thus अ + वप् + शप् + थास् ॥ The अ is grave by [[8.1.28]]. When it is not gravely accented, the अ drops. As यद्रुद्रेभ्योऽवपथाः ॥ Here अ is not grave by virtue of [[8.1.30]].
