For the adverb आ , is substituted in the छन्दस् the nasalised आँ , when a vowel follows it, and it retains its original form.
Thus अभ्र आं अपः (<i>Rig</i> V. 48.1); गभीर आं उग्रपुत्रे जिघांसतः (<i>Rig</i> VIII.67.11). Some read the word बहुलं into this <i>sutra</i>. Hence there is coalescence here: इन्द्रो बाहुभ्यामातरत् = आ अतरत् ॥
