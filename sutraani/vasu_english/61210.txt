In the मन्त्राः , these words जुष्ट and अर्पित have always the acute on the first syllable.
Thus जु꣡ष्ट देवानाम꣡र्पितं पितॄणाम् ॥ Some say that this rule applies only to जुष्ट and not to अर्पित; in which option is allowed even in the <i>Mantra</i> : so that it has acute on the last in the <i>Mantra</i> even : e.g.तस्मिन्साकं त्रिशता न शंकवोर्पिता꣡ ॥
