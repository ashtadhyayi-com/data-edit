The acute accent is on the first syllable of ईड् , वन्द , वृ , शंस् and दुह् , when they are followed by the affix ण्यत्।
Thus ई꣡ड्यम्, व꣡न्द्यम्, वा꣡र्यम्, श꣡स्यम्, दो꣡ह्या धेनुः ॥ The two letters ण् and त् being indicatory, the '<i>nyat</i>' is not included in '<i>yat</i>' of the last <i>sutra</i>. The accent would be regulated by त् ॥ The accent of त् however is debarred by this rule. The वृ in the <i>sutra</i> is वृङ् संभक्तौ of <i>Kriyadi</i> class: the वृञ् of स्वादि class takes <i>kyap</i> affix. See [[3.1.109]].
