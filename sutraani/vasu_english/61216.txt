The acute accent is optionally on the first syllables of त्याग , राग , हास , कुह , श्वठ , and क्रथ।
Thus त्या꣡गः or त्यागः꣡, रा꣡गः, रागः꣡, हा꣡सः, हासः꣡ ॥ These are formed by घञ् affix and by [[6.1.159]] would take acute on the final, this ordains acute on the first syllable also. कु꣡हः or कुहः꣡, श्व꣡ठः or श्वठः, क्रथः॒ or क्र॒थः formed by अच् [[3.1.13]].
