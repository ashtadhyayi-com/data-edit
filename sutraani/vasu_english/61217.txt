What is formed by an affix having an indicatory र , has acute on the penultimate syllable, the full word consisting of more than two syllables.
A penultimate syllable can be only in a word consisting of three syllables or more. Thus करणी꣡यम् and हरणी꣡यम् formed by अनीयर् [[3.1.96]]; पटुजाती꣡यः, मृदुजाती꣡यः by जातीयर् [[5.3.19]]. This debars [[3.1.3]].
