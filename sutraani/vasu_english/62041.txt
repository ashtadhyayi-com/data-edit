The word गो retains its accent before 1. साद 2. सादि and 3. सारथि।
Thus गोःसादः or गां सादयति = गो꣡सादः, गोः सादिः गो꣡ सादिः, and गो꣡ सारथिः ॥ साद is formed from सद् with the affix घञ् and forms a Genitive compound (गोः सादः). Or from the causative verb सादयति, we get गोसादः by adding अण् [[3.9.1]] गोसादी is formed by णिनि from the same causative root. The <i>Krit</i>-accent is debarred in the case of साद and सादिन्, the <i>Samasa</i>-accent [[6.1.223]] in the case of सारथि ॥
