The word राजन् optionally retains its original accent in a कर्मधारय समास before the words ब्राह्मण and कुमार।
Thus रा꣡जब्राह्मणः or राजब्राह्मणा꣡, रा꣡जकुमारः or राजकुमारः꣡ ॥ The word राजन् is formed by the affix कनिन् added to राज् (<i>Unadi</i> I.156). But राज्ञो ब्राह्मण = राजब्राह्मणः where the compound is not <i>Karmadharaya</i>. The making of this a separate aphorism is for the purpose of preventing the <i>yathasankhya</i> rule and also for the sake of the subsequent <i>sutra</i> into which the <i>anuvritti</i> of राजन् runs and not of आर्य ॥
