The word ग्राम when first member of a compound optionally retains its original accent before a word denoting 'a professional man or artisan'.
Thus ग्रामनापितः or ग्रामनापितः, ग्रामकुलालः or ग्रामकुलालः ॥ The word ग्राम has acute on the first. Why do we say "ग्राम"? Observe परमनापितः ॥ Why do we say 'a <i>silpi</i> a professional workman'? Observe ग्रामरथ्या; where there is no option.
