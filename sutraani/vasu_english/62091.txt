The following words do not get acute accent (उदात्त) on the first syllable when standing before अर्म etc. - 1. भूत 2. अधिक 3. संजीव 4. मद्र 5. अश्मन् and 6. कञ्जल।
Thus भूतार्म꣡म्, अधिकार्म꣡म्, संजीवार्म꣡म्, मद्रार्म꣡म्, अश्मार्म꣡म्, मद्राश्मार्मम् (because the <i>sutra</i> shows the compounding of those words in <i>madrasmam</i>) कञ्जलार्मम् ॥ All these compounds have acute on the final by [[6.1.223]].

Vart:- In the Vedas the words दिवोदास &c, have acute on the first syllable. Thus दिवोदासाय गायत, वध्य्रश्वाय दाशुषे ॥
