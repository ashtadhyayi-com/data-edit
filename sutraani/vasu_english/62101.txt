But when the words 1. हास्तिन 2. फलक and 3. मार्देय precede पुर , the acute accent (उदात्त) does not fall on their final.
This is an exception to [[6.2.99]]. Thus हास्तिनपुरम्, फलकपुरम्, मार्देयपुरम् ॥ The son of मृदु is मार्देय formed by ढक्, the word belonging to <i>Subhradi</i> class. The उ is elided by [[6.4.147]].
