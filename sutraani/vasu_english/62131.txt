At the end of a non-कर्मधारय तत्-पुरुष समास the words वर्ग्य etc. have acute accent (उदात्त) on the first syllable.
Thus वासुदेवव꣡र्ग्यः, वासुदेवप꣡क्ष्यः, अर्जुनव꣡र्ग्यः, अर्जुनप꣡क्ष्यः ॥ In a <i>Karmadharaya</i> we have परमवर्ग्यः ॥ The words वर्ग्य &c are no where exhibited as such; the primitive words वर्ग, पूग, गण &c subdivision of दिगादि [[4.3.54]] are here referred to, as ending with यत् affix.
