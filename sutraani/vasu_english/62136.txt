The word कुण्ड at the end of a तत्-पुरुष समास denoting 'a wood or forest' has acute accent (उदात्त) on the first syllable.
The word कुण्ड here denotes 'a wood' by metaphor. Thus दर्भकु꣡ण्डम्, शरकु꣡ण्डम् ॥ Why do we say when denoting 'a wood'? Observe मृतकुण्डम् ॥ The word कुण्ड means (1) a basin (2) a caste called <i>kunda</i>. Some say it means 'forest' also primarily and metaphorically. The force here is that of समुदाय, i.e. शरवणसमुदायः = शरकुण्डम् ॥
