The word भगाल at the end of a तत्-पुरुष समास preserves its original accent.
The synonyms of भगाल are also included. As कुम्भीभगा꣡लम्, कुम्भीकपा꣡लम्, कुम्भीनदा꣡लम् ॥ The words भगाल &c, have acute on the middle. <i>Phit</i> II.9. The word प्रकृत्य governs the subsequent <i>sutras</i> upto [[6.2.143]].
