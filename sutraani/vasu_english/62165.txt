In a बहुव्रीहि समास ending in मित्र and अजिन the acute accent (उदात्त) falls on the last syllable when the compound denotes a Name.
As देवमित्रः꣡, ब्रह्ममित्रः꣡, वृकाजिनः꣡, कुलाजिनः꣡ कृष्णाजिनः꣡ ॥ Why do we say 'a Name'? Observe प्रियमित्रः꣡, महाजिनः ॥

Vart:- Prohibition must be stated in the case of मित्र when the name is that of a <i>Rishi</i>. As विश्वामि꣡त्रः which is governed by [[6.2.106]] ॥
