In a बहुव्रीहि समास formed by the negative particle नञ् or by सु and ending in the affix कप् -- नद्यृतश्च [[5.4.153]], has acute accent (उदात्त) on the syllable preceding the affix.
By the last <i>sutra</i> the accent would have fallen on कप्, this makes it fall on the vowel preceding it. Thus अकुमारीको꣡देशः, अवृषली꣡कः, अब्रह्मबन्धू꣡कः, सुकुमा꣡रीकः, सुवृष꣡लीकः, सुब्रह्मबन्धू꣡कः ॥
