After a preposition वन has acute accent (उदात्त) on the final in compounds of every kind.
Thus प्रवणे꣡ यष्टव्यम्, निर्वणे꣡ प्रणिधीयते, the न changed to ण by [[8.4.5]]. The word '<i>samasa</i>' is used in the <i>sutra</i> to indicate that all sorts of compounds are meant, otherwise only <i>Bahuvrihi</i> would have been meant.
