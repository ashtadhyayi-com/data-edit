In a तत्-पुरुष समास the word उत्पुच्छ may optionally have acute accent (उदात्त) on the final.
Thus उत्क्रान्तः पुच्छात् = उत्पुच्छः꣡ or उ꣡त्पुच्छः [[6.2.2]] ॥ When this word is derived by the affix अच् from पुच्छमुदस्यति = उत्पुच्छयति, then it would always have taken acute on the final by [[6.2.144]], the present <i>sutra</i> ordains option there also. The rule does not apply to a non-<i>Tatpurusha</i>: as, उदस्तं पुच्छमस्य = उत्पुच्छः ॥
