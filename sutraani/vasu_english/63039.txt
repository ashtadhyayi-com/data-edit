The feminine is not changed into masculine when it is formed by such a तद्धित affix which causes the वृद्धि of the first syllable with exception however of तद्धित-substitute meaning 'coloured therewith' and 'made thereof'.
Thus स्रौघ्नीभार्यः, माथुरीभार्यः, स्रौघ्नीपाशा, माथुरीपाशा, स्रौघ्नीयते, माथुरीयते, स्रौघ्नीमानिनी and माथुरीमानिनी ॥

Why do we says 'which causes <i>Vriddhi</i>'? Observe मध्यमभार्यः (मध्ये भवा = मध्यमा formed by the affix म [[4.3.8]] which does not cause <i>Vriddhi</i>). Why do we say 'of a <i>Taddhita</i>'? Observe काण्डलावभार्यः (काण्डं लुनाति = काण्डलावी with अण् (कर्मण्यण्) and ङीप्). Why do we say when not meaning 'colored there with' or 'made thereof'? Observe कषायेण रक्ता = काषायी, काषायी वृहतिका यस्य = काषायवृहतिकः, लोहस्य विकारोलौही लौही ईषा यस्य स्थस्य = लोहेषः [[4.3.134]]. The word वृद्धिनिमित्तस्य should be explained as a <i>Bahuvrihi</i>, and not a <i>Tatpurusha</i>, i. e. a <i>taddhita</i> affix, in which there is an element like ण् or ञ् or क् &c which causes <i>Vriddhi</i>. Therefore this exception does not apply to तावद्भार्यः यावद्भार्यः (तावती भार्या यस्य &c). Here the affix वतुप् [[5.2.39]] added to तद् does not cause <i>Vriddhi</i> by its own force, but by [[6.3.91]].
