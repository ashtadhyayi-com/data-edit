The word सह retains its original form when the sentence denotes benediction, except when it is गो , वत्स or हल।
Thus स्वस्ति देवदत्ताय सहपुत्राय, सहच्छात्राय, सहामात्याय ॥ But optionally here, as स्वस्ति भवते सहगवे or सगवे, सहवत्साय or सवत्साय, सहहलाय, or सहलाय ॥ The phrase अगोवत्सहलेषु is no part of the original <i>sutra</i>, but has been added by the <i>Kasika</i> from a <i>Vartika</i>.
