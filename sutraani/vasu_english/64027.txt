The penultimate nasal of रञ्ज् is elided before घञ् when the word formed with it expresses a state or an instrument.
Thus रागः 'passion, color, or the coloring stuff'. Thus आश्चर्यो रागः, विचित्र रागः denote भाव, while रज्यतेऽनेनिति रागः denotes instrument. The घञ् is added by [[3.3.121]]; and च् changed to क् by [[7.3.52]]. But रङ्गः = रजन्ति तस्मिन् 'a theatre'.
