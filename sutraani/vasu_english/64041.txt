A stem ending in a nasal and followed by the affix विट् or वन् always substitutes for long आ for its nasal.
Thus अब्जाः, गोजाः, ऋतजाः, अद्रिजाः, गोषाः (e. g. गोषा इन्द्रोनृषा असि <i>Rig</i> V.), कूपखाः, शतखाः, सहस्रखाः, दधिकाः, अग्रेगा उन्नेतणाम् ॥ The affix विट् (which is totally elided) is added under [[3.2.67]]. The स of सन् is changed to ष by [[8.3.108]]. in गोषा ॥ With the affix वन् we have the following :-- विजावा, अग्रेजावा [[3.2.75]]. The repetition of the word अनुनासिक in this <i>sutra</i> shows that the limitation of <i>anudattopadesa</i> &c. which applied to the अनुनासिक of <i>sutra</i> [[6.4.37]] does not apply here.
