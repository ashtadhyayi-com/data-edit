The इ of the verbal अङ्ग (stem) formed with the affix णि is elided before an आर्धधातुक affix which does not take the augment इट् ।
This debars इयङ्, the semi-vowel य, the <i>guna</i>, <i>Vriddhi</i> and the long substitutions. Thus अततक्षत्, अररक्षत्, आशिशत्, कारणा, हारणा, कारकः, हारकः, कार्यते, हार्यते and ज्ञीप्सति (see (VII.4. I) for the shortening of the stem of the Aorists in these). Why do we say 'not having the augment इट्?' Observe कारयिता and हारयिता ॥
