अय् is substituted for the इ of णि before the affixes 1. आम् 2. अन्त 3. आलु 4. आय्य 5. इत्नु and 6. इष्णु।
Thus कारयां चकार, हारयां चकार, गण्डयन्तः, मण्डयन्तः ( formed by the <i>Unadi</i> affix झच्, झ = अन्त, added to the roots गंड and मंड) आलु । स्पृहयालुः । आय्य, स्पृहयाय्यः, गृहयाय्यः ॥ इत्नुः - स्तनायत्नुः ॥ इष्णुः - पोषयिष्णुः ॥ Thus <i>sutra</i> could have been well dispensed with; for the इ of णि would take <i>guna</i> ए which will be changed to अय् by the rules of <i>Sandhi</i>, before these affixes. This substitution of अय् for इ is for the sake of the subsequent <i>sutra</i> however, because there इ could not be changed to अय by any <i>sandhi</i>-rules.
