ए is substituted for the आ of the above roots (1. मा 2. स्था 3. गा 4. पा 5. हा (जुहाति) and 6. सा (सो)) in the Benedictive Mood Active.
Thus देयात्, मेयात्, धेयात्, स्थेयात्, गेयात्, पेयात् and अवसेयात् ॥ Before non-कित् and non-ङित् we have दासीष्ट and धासीष्ट ॥ By the word लिङ् is here meant the आशीर्लिङ् or the Precative mood; the Personal endings of which mood are <i>ardhadhatuka</i> by [[3.4.116]]. More-over by [[3.4.104]] the <i>Parasmaipada</i> affixes only of the Benedictive are कित्, so the present rule does not apply to the <i>Atmanepada</i> affixes of the Precative.
