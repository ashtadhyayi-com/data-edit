The आट् augment is found in the छन्दस् (Vedas) also.
It is found there before the roots beginning with a vowel as well as before consonant roots. Thus आवः, आनक्, and आयुनक् ॥ आवः is the Aorist of वृञ्, the affix being elided by [[2.4.80]]. आनक् from नश् [[2.4.80]], and आयुनक् is the Imperfect of युञ् ॥
