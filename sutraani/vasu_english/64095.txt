The penultimate of the Causative of ह्लद् (ह्लादि) is shortened before the Participle affixes क्त and क्तवतु।
Thus प्रह्लन्नः, प्रह्लन्नवान्, but प्रह्लादयति before non-<i>nishtha</i>. The rule applies also when क्तिन् follows as प्रह्लत्तिः ॥ This is done by splitting up the aphorism into two (1) ह्लादः (2) निष्ठायाम् ॥
