The elision of उ is invariable before व् and म् in the case of कृ 'to make' (करोति).
Thus कुर्वः and कुर्मः ॥ Thus कृ + उ + व = कुर् + उ + वः [[6.4.110]] = कुर् + ० + वः [[6.4.108]]. Now the <i>lopa</i> being not <i>sthanivat</i>, when a vowel is to be lengthened [[1.1.58]], उ of कु should be lengthened by [[8.2.77]]. This contingency is prevented by [[8.2.79]].
