Before a सार्वधातुक कित् or ङित् affix beginning with a consonant, the आ of श्ना (the विकरण of the क्र्यादिगणः (9th Conjugation)) and of the reduplicate अङ्ग (stems) is replaced by ई except when the root is दा 'to give' or धा (धु).
Thus लुनीतः, पुनीतः, लुनीथः, पुनीथः, लुनीते and पुनीते ॥ Of reduplicates we have मिमीते, मिमीषे, मिमीध्वे, संजिहीते, संजिहीषे, संजिहीध्वे ॥  Why do we say 'beginning with a consonant'? Observe लुनन्ति, मिमते ॥ Why with the exception of घु? Observe दत्तः, धत्तः ॥ So also लुनाति and जहाति before non-कित् and non-ङित् affixes.
