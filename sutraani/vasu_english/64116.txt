इ is optionally substituted for the आ of हा (जुहोत्यादिगणः (3rd Conjugation)) before a सार्वधातुक affix कित् or ङित् beginning with a consonant.
Thus जहितः or जहीतः [[6.4.113]] जहिथः or जहीथः [[6.4.113]]. But जहति before a vowel affix, and जहाति before a non-ङित् affix, an हीयते and जिहीयते before an <i>ardhadhatuka</i>. The separation of this <i>sutra</i> from the last, is for the sake of subsequent aphorisms.
