Whatever will be taught in the following up to the end of this अध्याय should be understood to apply to the nominal stems called भ -- यचि भम् [[1.4.18]], तसौ मत्वर्थे [[1.4.19]].
Thus <i>sutra</i> [[6.4.130]] says 'पद् is the substitute of पाद्' ॥ It means पद् is substituted for पाद् when the latter gets the designation of भ ॥ Thus द्विपदः पश्य, द्विपदा कृतम् but द्विपादौ, दिपादः where the affixes are those of <i>Sarvanamasthana</i>.
