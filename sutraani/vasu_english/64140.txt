The final आ of a भ अङ्ग (stem) is elided when it ends in a root.
Thus कीलालपः पश्य, कीलालपा, कीलालपे, शुभंयः पश्य, शुभंया, and शुभंये from कीलालपा and शुभंया ॥ Why do we say 'the आ of a root'? Observe खट्वाः पश्य, मालाः पश्य ॥ This <i>sutra</i> should be divided into two (1) आतः (2) धातोः; so that the long आ in general may be elided. For <i>Panini</i> himself has declined क्त्वा and श्ना by the elision of आ, see [[7.1.37]], (क्त्वो ल्यप्) and [[3.1.83]], (श्नः शानच्) ॥
