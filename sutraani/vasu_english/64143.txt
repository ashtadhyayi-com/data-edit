Before an affix having an indicatory ड् , the last vowel, with the consonant, if any, that follows it, is elided.
Thus कुमुद्वत्, नड्वत् and वेतस्वत् with ड्मतुप् [[4.2.87]]. So also त्रिंशता क्रीतः = त्रिंशकः with ड्वुन् of [[5.1.24]]. The rule applies even to non-<i>bha</i> bases, for effect must be given to the indicatory ड ॥ Thus उपसरजः, मन्दुरजः [[3.2.97]] with the <i>krit</i> affix उ) ॥
