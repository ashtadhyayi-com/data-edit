The य् of a तद्धित affix when preceded by a consonant and penultimate in a अङ्ग (stem), is elided before the feminine ई।
The <i>anuvritti</i> of "<i>taddhita</i>" ceases, because of its mention in the next <i>sutra</i>. Hence this <i>sutra</i> applies to feminine ई only. As गार्गी, वात्सी from गार्ग्य and वात्स्य ॥ Why do we say 'when preceded by a consonant'? Observe कारिकेयी ॥ Why do we say 'of a <i>Taddhita</i>'. Observe वैद्यस्य भार्या = वैद्यी ॥
