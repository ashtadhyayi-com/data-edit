As अक्षी꣡ ते इन्द्रपिङ्गले कपेरिव ॥ अक्षी꣡भ्यान् ते नासिकाभ्याम् ॥ In अक्षी the augment नुम् is not added to the stem before the vowel-beginning ending, because [[7.1.73]], which ordained नुम्, is superseded by the present <i>sutra</i>, and being once superseded, it is superseded for good. (सकृद् गतौ विप्रतिषेधे यद्बाधितं तद्बाधितमेव) ॥
