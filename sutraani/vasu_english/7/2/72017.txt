Thus मिन्नमनेन or मेदितमनेन, प्रमिन्नः, प्रमेदितः ॥ The <i>Saunagas</i> optionally make the <i>Nishtha</i> set after the root शक्, when the affix has a Passive significance even, as शकितो घटः कर्त्तुम् or शक्तोः घटः कर्त्तुम् ॥ Not so, when the affix has Impersonal force, as शक्तमनेन् ॥ The root अस् 'to throw' (अस्यति), is followed by सेट् <i>Nishtha</i>, when the sense is Impersonal: as असितमनेन; but not when the beginning of action is meant, as अस्तः काण्डः ॥
