Thus लविता, लवितुम्, लवितव्यम्, पविता, पवितुम्, पवितव्यम् ॥ Why '<i>ardhadhatuka</i>'? Observe आस्ते, वस्ते ॥ The <i>niyama</i> rule of [[7.2.76]] ordaining इट् augment to <i>sarvadhatuka</i> affixes of <i>Rudadi</i> verbs, would prevent इट् augment before <i>sarvadhatuka</i> affixes when coming after other roots. The employment of <i>ardhadhatuka</i> here can be dispensed with. Why before affixes beginning with a वल्-consonant? Observe लव्यम्, पव्यम्, लवनीयम्, पवनीयम् ॥ Though the <i>anuvritti</i> of इट् was understood here, its repetition is for the sake of preventing the prohibition of the foregoing <i>sutras</i> like [[7.2.8]].
