As अग्निमारुती in अग्निमारुतीमनड्वाहीमालभेत् ॥ आग्निमारुतं कर्म ॥ The rule applies to <i>Dvandas</i> relating to hymns (सूक्त) and sacrificial offerings (हवि). Therefore, not here, स्कान्दविशाखौ देवतेऽस्य = स्कान्दविशाखः [[4.2.24]]. So also ब्राह्मप्रजापत्यम् by ण्य from ब्रह्मप्रजापती ॥ See [[6.3.26]].

The short इ in the आग्नि in आग्निमारुत, आग्निवारुणम् is by [[6.3.28]]. A compound relates to a <i>sukta</i>, which worships a <i>deva</i> through hymns; and that by which a '<i>havis</i>' is determined, is a compound relating to sacrificial offering.
