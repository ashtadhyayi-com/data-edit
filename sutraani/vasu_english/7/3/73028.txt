Thus प्रवाहणस्यापत्यं = प्रावाहणेयः or प्रवाहणेयः ॥ The affix ढक् [[4.1.123]], is added, similarly प्रवाहणेयी भार्याऽस्य = प्रवाहणेयीभार्यः [[6.3.39]], masculation prohibited)). Or we may translate the <i>sutra</i> as "Before the affix ढ, the vowel of प्र in प्रवाहण optionally gets <i>Vriddhi</i>", and omit the rest. The masculation will still be prohibited by [[6.3.41]], (जाते)
