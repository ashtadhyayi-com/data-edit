This is an exception to [[7.3.52]]. Thus समाजः, उदाजः, परिव्राजः and परिव्राज्यम् ॥ There is no example of अज् with the affix ण्यत्, because by [[2.4.56]], वी replaces अज् before all <i>ardhadhatuka</i> affixes except घञ् and अप् ॥ The च of the <i>sutra</i> implies that the rule applies to other roots also not mentioned, as वाजः, वाज्यम् from वज् ॥
