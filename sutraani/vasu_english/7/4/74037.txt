In the छन्दस् (Vedas) long आ is substituted for the final of 1. अश्व and 2. अघ before the Denominative क्यच् ।
As अश्वायन्तो मघवन् (<i>Rig</i> VII. 32. 23), मा त्वा वृका अघायवो विदन् ॥ This also indicates that other words do not lengthen their vowel in the <i>Veda</i> before क्यच्, as taught in [[7.4.35]]. See <i>Vartika</i> to (III. I. 8). The word अघायु occurs in <i>Rig</i> I. 120.7, 27; 3.
