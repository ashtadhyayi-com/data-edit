अत् is the substitute of झ after a reduplicated verbal अङ्ग (stem).
As द꣡दति, ददतु, दधति, दधतु; जक्षति, जक्षतुः जाग्रति, जाग्रतु ॥ This अत् is replaced by जुस्, as अददुः, अदधुः ॥  Here also the accent will be on the first syllable of the substitute. Thus अ꣡ति, अ꣡त, अ꣡ते, अ꣡तु, अ꣡ताम् in the subsequent <i>sutras</i>. The accent of the reduplicates, however, is governed by [[6.1.189]], (अभ्यस्तानामादिः) ॥
