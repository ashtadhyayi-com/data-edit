After a neutral अङ्ग (stem), इ is substituted for the endings (जस् and शस्) of Nominative 1st-Case and Accusative -2nd case.
Thus कुण्डानि तिष्ठन्ति, कुण्डानि पश्य, दधिनी, मधूनि, त्रपूणि, जतूनि ॥ The augment न् is by [[7.1.72]]. The word शस् in the <i>sutra</i> being read along with जस् denotes the Accusative Plural ending शस् and not the <i>Taddhita</i> affix शस् [[5.4.43]]; as कुण्डशो ददाति, वनशः प्रविशति ॥
