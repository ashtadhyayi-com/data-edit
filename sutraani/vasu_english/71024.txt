After a neutral अङ्ग (stem) अ , अम् is substituted for सु and अम् , the Nominative 1st-Case and Accusative -2nd case endings.
As कुण्डं तिष्ठति, कुण्डं पश्य ॥ So also वनम्, पीठम् ॥ Why do we not say "म् is substituted"? कुण्ड + म् = कुण्डाम्, thus there would be lengthening if only म् was taught [[7.3.101]], while कुण्ड + अम् = कुण्डम् (the single substitution of the form of the antecedent by [[6.1.107]].
