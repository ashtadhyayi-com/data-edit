For the personal ending अम् of the 1st person singular (in the Aorist) म -- अनेकाल्शित्सर्वस्य [[1.1.55]] is substituted in the छन्दस् (Vedas).
The अम् here is the substitution of मिप्, and not the accusative singular affix. As वर्धी वृत्रम् (for अवधिपम्) <i>Rig</i> I.165.8: क्रमीम् वृक्षस्य शाखाम् ॥ The अट् augment is diversely elided [[6.4.75]]. The indicatory श् of मश् shows that the whole of the affix अम् is to be replaced. The substitution of म् for म् is to prevent the change of  म् into <i>anusvara</i> as in [[8.3.25]].
