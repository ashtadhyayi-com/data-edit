In the छन्दस् (Vedas) the Absolutive affix क्त्वा gets at the end the augment य।
Thus दत्वाय सविता धियः (for दत्वा), This <i>sutra</i> is not read immediately after [[7.1.38]], as in that <i>sutra</i>, the <i>anuvritti</i> of <i>samasa</i> is understood, while there is no such <i>anuvritti</i> here.
