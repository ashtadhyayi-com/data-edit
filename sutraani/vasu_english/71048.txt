In the छन्दस् (Vedas) the Absolutive इष्ट्वीनम् is irregularly formed for इष्ट्वा।
To the root यज् is added that क्त्वा, and the final आ is replaced by ईनम् ॥ As इष्ट्वीनम् देवान् for इष्ट्वा देवान् ॥ The  च in the <i>sutra</i> indicates that there are other forms like this, as पीत्वीनम् for पीत्वा ॥
