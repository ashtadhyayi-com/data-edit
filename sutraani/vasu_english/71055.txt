The augment न् is added before the Genitive 6th-Case plural ending आम् after the numerals called षष् and after चतुर्।
As षण्णाम्, पञ्चानाम्, [[6.4.7]], सप्तानाम्, नवानाम्, दशानाम्, चतुर्णाम् ॥ A numeral ending in र् is not '<i>shash</i>', hence the specific mention of चतुर ॥

This rule applies to compounds ending with these numerals, when these latter are the principal member, as परमषण्णाम्, परमपंचानाम्, परमचतुर्णाम्, but, प्रियषषाम्, प्रियपञ्चाम्, प्रियचतुराम् where the Numerals are secondary (<i>upasarjana</i>).
