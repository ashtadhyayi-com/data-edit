After गो when standing at the end of a ऋक् verse, the augment न् comes before the Genitive 6th-Case plural आम् ।
As विद्माहि त्वा सत्पतिं शूरगोनाम्; but गवां गोत्रमुदसृजो यदङ्गिरः in the beginning of a <i>Pada</i>. "All rules have exceptions in the Vedas" is an established maxim, so at the end of a <i>Pada</i>, sometimes this rule does not apply, as हन्तारं शत्रूणां कृधि विराजं गोपतिं गवाम् ॥
