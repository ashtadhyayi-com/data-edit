The augment नुम् is added to लभ् 'to get' preceded by a Preposition before the affixes खल् -- ईषद्दुःसुषु कृच्छ्राकृच्छ्रार्थेषु खल् [[3.3.126]], and घञ्।
Thus ईषत्प्रलम्भः, सुप्रलम्भः, दुष्प्रलम्भः, प्रलम्भः, विप्रलम्भः ॥ This is a <i>Niyama</i> rule, and restricts the scope of [[7.1.64]]. Before the vowel affixes खल् and घञ्, the root लभ् gets the augment नुम् only then when it is preceded by a Preposition, and not otherwise, as ईषल्लभः, लाभो वर्तते ॥
