औ is substituted for the final of दिव् before the affix सु (Nominative 1st-Case and Vocative Case singular).
As द्यौः ॥ There is a nominal-stem दिव् which is taken here. It has no indicatory letters annexed to it. The root दिव् is not to be taken here, as it has the indicatory letter उ and is exhibited in the <i>Dhatupatha</i> as  दिवु ॥ The nominal-stem derived from दिवु, does not take औ, but ऊ, the Nominative Singular of which is द्युः as अक्षद्युः (See [[6.4.19]], and [[6.1.131]].
