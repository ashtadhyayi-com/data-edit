For the final long ॠ of a root, there is substituted इर् -- उरण् रपरः [[1.1.51]].
As किरति, गिरति from कॄ and गॄ of the <i>Tudadi</i> class. आस्तीर्णम् विस्तीर्णम् from स्तॄ, the lengthening by [[8.2.77]]. Why do we say of a root? Observe पितॄणाम्, मातॄणाम् ॥

This substitution will apply to Derivative roots also, as चिकीर्षति from कॄ 'to scatter'.
