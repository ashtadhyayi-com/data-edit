Before an इट् beginning substituted-Aorist of the परस्मैपद there is optional वृद्धि of the vowel of ऊर्णु।
As प्रौर्णावीत् or प्रौर्णवीत् ॥ This option applies when the सिच् is not treated as ङित् ॥ But after ऊर्णु, सिच् is optionally ङित्, [[1.2.6]]: when it is ङित्, there being neither <i>guna</i> nor <i>vriddhi</i>; we have उवङ् substitution, as प्रौर्णुवीत् [[6.4.77]].
