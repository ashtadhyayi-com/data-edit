The participial affixes त and तवत् (क्त and क्तवतु) do not get the इट् augment after श्वि and after the root which has an indicatory ई।
As शूनः, शूनवान; ओलेजी (VI.I0), - लग्न, लग्नवान्; ओविजी (VI.9), औद्विग्नः, उद्विग्नवान् ॥ The त is changed to न because of the indicatory ओ [[8.2.45]]. So also दीपी (IV. 42), दीप्तः, दीप्तवान् ॥ In the <i>Dhatupatha</i>, डीङ् (IV. 27), is classed among ओदित् roots, and it indicates that the <i>Nishtha</i> is <i>anit</i> after it : and ओ is for न-change [[8.2.45]] as, उड्डीनः, उड्डीनवान् ॥ The word निष्ठायाम् governs the following <i>sutras</i> upto [[7.2.35]].
