A वलादि आर्धधातुक affix optionally takes इट् after कुष् when it is preceded by निर्।
As निष्कोष्टा or निष्कोषिता, निष्कोष्टुम् or निष्कोषितुम्, निष्कोष्टव्यम् or निष्कोषितव्यम् ॥ But only कोषिता, कोषितुं, कोषितव्यम् without निर् ॥ The exhibition of निर् instead of निस् indicates the existence of a separate and distinct preposition निर्, besides निस् ॥ It is the र् of this निर् which is changed to ल् by [[8.2.19]], in निलयनम्; for the र् of निस् being <i>asiddha</i> could not be changed to ल् ॥
