The affixes क्त्वा , क्त and क्त्वातु get the इट् augment after the root लुभ् (तुदादिगणः (6th Conjugation)) when meaning 'entangled of confused'.
As लुभित्वा and लोभित्वा, विलुभिताः केशाः, विलुभितः सीमन्तः, विलुभितानि पदानि ॥ विमोहनं = आकुलीकरणं ॥ By [[7.2.48]] लुभ would have optionally caused इट् to come before <i>ktva</i>, and then by [[7.2.15]] the <i>Nishtha</i> would never have been सेट् ॥ Hence this <i>sutra</i>. Why do we say when meaning 'to entangle'? See लुब्धः वृषलः = शीतेन पीडितः, ॥ लुभ् 'to be greedy' (<i>Divadi</i> 128) is not governed by this rule, but by [[7.2.48]].
