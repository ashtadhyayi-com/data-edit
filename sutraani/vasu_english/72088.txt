आ is substituted for the final of युष्मद् and अस्मद् before the case-ending of the Nominative 1st-Case dual in the secular literature.
As युवाम्, आवाम् ॥ Why of the nominative? Observe युवयोः, आवयोः ॥ Why in the Dual ? Observe त्वं, अहं, यूयं, वयं ॥ Why in the secular literature ? Observe
युवं वस्त्राणि पीवसा वसाथे in the <i>Veda</i>, so also आवम् ॥
