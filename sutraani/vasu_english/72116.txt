In a अङ्ग (stem) ending in a consonant with an अ immediately preceding it, the वृद्धि is substituted for such अ when followed by an indicatory ञ् or ण्।
As पाकः, त्यागः, यागः with घञ्, पाचि with the causative णि, पाचकः with ण्वुल् ॥ Why do we say 'अ'? Observe भेदयति, भेदकः with <i>guna</i> only from भिद् where इ is penultimate and not अ ॥ Why do we say 'penultimate'? Observe चकासयति, तक्षकः ॥
