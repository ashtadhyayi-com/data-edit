A root-अङ्ग (stem) ending in आ gets the augment युक् (य्) before the Aorist sign चिण् which causes also the वृद्धि of the root-vowel.
As अदायि, अधायि with चिण्, दायः and दायकः, धायः, धायकः with ण and ण्वुल् ॥ Why चिण् and <i>krit</i> only? Observe ददौ, दधौ in the Perfect, and चौडिः, बालाकिः with इञ् a <i>Taddhita</i> affix [[4.1.96]]. So also ज्ञा देवता अस्य = ज्ञः ॥
