Before a case-ending beginning with भ् or स् (literally a consonant of the झल् प्रत्यहार) in the plural, ए is substituted for the final अ of Nominal-stem.
As वृक्षेभ्यः, प्लक्षेभ्यः, वृक्षेषु, प्लक्षेषु ॥ Why in the Plural? Observe वृक्षाभ्याम् प्लक्षाभ्याम् ॥ Why before a case-affix beginning with a झल् consonant? Observe वृक्षाणाम् (the lengthening here is by [[6.4.3]]). Why a case-affix? Observe यजध्वम्, पचध्वम् ॥
