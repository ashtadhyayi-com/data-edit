The augment याट् is added to the case-endings of the Dative -4th Case, Ablative -5th case and Genitive 6th-Case singular after a feminine अङ्ग (stem) ending in आ।
As खट्वायै, बहुराजायै, कारीषगन्ध्यायै, खट्वायाः, बहुराजायाः, कारीषगन्ध्यायाः ॥ But in the compound अतिखट्व, (from खट्वामतिक्रान्तः), this rule does not apply, on the maxim ङ्याब् ग्रहणेऽदीर्घः as the आ has been shortened here. Even when the word अतिखट्व assumes the form अतिखट्वा in the Dative by [[7.3.102]], the affix does not take the augment याट्, because this long आ is a <i>lakshanika</i> आ only, while the आ of the <i>sutra</i> is a <i>pratipadokta</i> (लक्षणप्रतिपदोक्तयोः प्रतिपदस्यैव) ॥
