A long is substituted for the final vowel of the अङ्ग (stem) before the Adverbial affix च्वि -- अभूततद्भावे कृभ्वस्तियोगे सम्पद्यकर्तरि च्विः [[5.4.50]].
As शुची करोति, शुची स्यात्, शुची भवति, पटू करोति, पटू स्यात्, पटू भवति ॥ The च draws in the <i>anuvritti</i> of the phrase 'non-<i>krit</i>, and non-<i>sarvadhatuka</i>' from the last <i>sutra</i>, which though not of any direct use in this aphorism, is necessary for the sake of subsequent ones. See [[7.4.32]] for words ending in अ ॥
