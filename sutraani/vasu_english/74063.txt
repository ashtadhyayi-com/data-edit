The Palatal is not substituted for the Guttural of the reduplicate of कु (कवते) in the intensive.
As कोकूयते उष्ट्रः, कोकूयते खरः ॥ The कु here is कुङ् (<i>Bhuadi</i> 999), and not कु 'to make sound' (<i>Adadi</i> 33, कौति), nor कुङ् (<i>Tudadi</i> 108, कुवति) ॥ Of those two, we have चोकूयते ॥ Why do we say in the Intensive? Observe चुकुवे ॥
