गुण is substituted for the final of इ and उ (with their long) of a reduplicate when the Intensive character यङ् follows and also when it is elided.
Thus चेचीयते, लोलूयते with यङ्, and जोहवीति with यङ् लुक् ॥ The ईट् is added in the latter by [[7.3.94]]. So also चोक्रुशीति from क्रुश् [[7.3.94]] and [[7.3.87]].
