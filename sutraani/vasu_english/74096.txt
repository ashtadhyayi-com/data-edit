Short अ is optionally substituted for the vowel of the reduplicate in the reduplicated Aorist of the Causative of वेष्ट् and चेष्ट्।
Thus अववेष्टत् or अविवेष्टत्, अचचेष्टत् and अचिचेष्टत् ॥ In one alternative there is shortening of the reduplicate [[7.4.59]], and after such shortening, there is अ substituted in the other alternative.
