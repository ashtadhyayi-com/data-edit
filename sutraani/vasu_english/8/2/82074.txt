 द् or रु may optionally be substituted for the स् of a root, , before the personal ending सि (स्) when such स् stands at the end of a word.
Thus अचकास् त्वम् or अचकात् त्वम्, अन्वशास् त्वम् or अन्वशात् त्वम् ॥ For the रु is first substituted <i>visarjaniya</i>, which is then changed to स् ॥

The word धातुः is employed in the <i>sutra</i> for the sake of the subsequent <i>sutras</i> : so also the word रु ॥
