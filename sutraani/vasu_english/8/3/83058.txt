The word व्यवाय 'separation, intervention' applies to every one of the words नुम्, &c. Thus (1) when नुम् intervenes, as सर्पींषि, यजूंषि, हवींषि [[7.1.72]], [[6.4.10]]. (2) When a <i>visarjaniya</i> intervenes, as सर्पिःषु, यजुःषु, हविःषु  [[8.3.36]] (3) When a Sibilant intervenes, as सर्पिष्षु, यजुष्षु हविष्षु ॥ 

The षत्व takes place, when नुम् &c intervene singly and not when they intervene collectively. Therefore not here, निंस्से, निंस्स्वे from the root निंस् 'to kiss'. Here there is the intervention of two, namely, नुम् and स् [[3.4.91]].

The word इण्कोः is in the Ablative case, and it required that the स् should follow immediately after it. Hence the necessity of the present <i>sutra</i> for the intervention of certain letters.
