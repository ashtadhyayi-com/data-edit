Thus त्रिभिष्टुतस्य or त्रिभिस्तुतस्य, गोष्टोमं षोडशिनम् or गोस्तोमं षोडशिनम् ॥ This change would have taken place by the general rule contained in the next <i>sutra</i> [[8.3.106]], the special mention of स्तुत and स्तोम here is a mere amplification. The word छन्दसि of this <i>sutra</i> governs the subsequent <i>sutra</i> also.
