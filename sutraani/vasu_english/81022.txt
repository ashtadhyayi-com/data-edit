For the Genitive 6th-Case and Dative -4th Case singular (of युष्मद् and अस्मद् are substituted) ते and मे respectively (when a word precedes and these substitutes are अनुदात्त).
Thus ग्रामस्ते स्वम्, ग्रामो मे स्वम्, ग्रामस्ते दीयते, ग्रामो मे दीयते ॥ For the Accusative singular, other substitutes have been taught in the next <i>sutra</i>, hence ते and मे come only in Genitive and Dative.
