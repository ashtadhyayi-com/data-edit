The स् is elided after the augment इट् if after this स् the augment ईट् follows.
Thus the Aorist-stem of लू is अलाविस् [[3.1.44]]; [[7.2.35]], the Personal ending त् gets the augment ईट् by [[7.3.96]]; and by the present <i>sutra</i> , this स् between इ and ई is dropped, and we have अलावीत्; so also अदावीत्, असेवीत्, अकोषीत्, अमोषीत् ॥

Why do we say 'after the augment इट्'? Observe अकार्षीत्, अहार्षीत् ॥ Why when the augment ईट् follows ? Observe अलाविष्टाम् अलाविषुः ॥
