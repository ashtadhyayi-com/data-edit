The निष्ठा त is changed to न after अञ्च् but not when it is in connection with an Ablative -5th case.
Thus समक्नौ शकुनेः पादौ = सङ्गतौ; तस्मात् पशवो न्यक्नाः ॥

Why do we say 'when not in construction with an Ablative case'? Observe उदत्तमुदकं कूपात् = उद्धृतं 'drawn out'.

The word व्यक्तम् is from the root अञ्जू व्यक्तिम्रक्षण कान्तिगतिषु, and not from the root अञ्च्; and hence the <i>Nishtha</i> is not changed.
