The vowel in ओम् is प्लुत in the beginning of a sacred text.
The word अभ्यादानं and means the commencement of a sacred <i>mantra</i> or Vedic text. Thus ओ३म् अ॒ग्निमीले॑ पु॒रोहि॑तं य॒ज्ञस्य॑ दे॒वमृ॒त्विजं॑ ॥ (<i>Rig</i> I.I.I).

Why do we say "in the beginning"? Observe ओमित्येतदक्षरमुद्गीथमुपासीत (<i>Chhandogya</i> <i>Upanishad</i>. I.1.1). Here ओम् is not used to indicate the commencement of a Text or <i>Mantra</i>, but is itself the subject of comment.
