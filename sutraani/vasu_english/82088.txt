The vowel of ये becomes प्लुत in a sacred text when it is used in a sacrificial work.
Thus ये३यजामहे ॥ Why do we say when employed in sacrificial work? Observe ये यजामह इति पञ्चाक्षरम् "<i>Ye</i> <i>yajamahe</i> consists of five syllables". Here it is simple recitation.

<i>Ishti</i>:- The word ये is <i>pluta</i> before यजामहे only. Therefore not so here: ये देवा॑सो दि॒व्येकाद॑शस्थ (<i>Rig</i>. I.139.11).
