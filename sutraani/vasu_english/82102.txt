In 1. उपरि 2. स्विद् and 3. आसीद् the end vowel is अनुदात्त and प्लुत।
The word <i>anudatta</i> is understood here also. The end-vowel would have been <i>pluta</i> here by [[8.2.97]]: the present <i>sutra</i> really ordains accent-lessness of this <i>pluta</i>.

Thus अधः स्विदासी३त्, उपरि स्विदासी३त् (<i>Rig</i> X. 129.5). In the first portion आसीत् is <i>pluta</i> and <i>udatta</i> by [[8.2.97]], in the second it is <i>anudatta</i> by the present <i>sutra</i>.
