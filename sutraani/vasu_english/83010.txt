रु is optionally substituted for the न् of नॄन् before प्।
The अ in प is for the sake of pronunciation only. Thus नॄँः पाहि or नॄंः पाहि; नॄँ प्रीणीहि or नॄँः प्रीणीहि ॥

Why do we say 'before प्'? Observe नॄन् भोजयति ॥ Some read the <i>anuvritti</i> of उभयथा into this <i>sutra</i>, so that it is an optional one. Thus we have नॄन् पाहि also. The nominative case in नॄन् has the force of Genitive.
