म् is substituted for म् of स्म् before the word राज् ending with the affix क्विप्।
Thus सम्राट्, साम्राज्यम् ॥ The substitution of म for म is for the sake of preventing the <i>anusvara</i> change (cf [[7.1.40]]). Why do we say 'before राज्'? See संयत् [[6.4.40]] <i>Vartika</i>). Why do we say 'of सम्'? Observe किं राट् [[5.4.70]]. Why do we say 'ending with क्विप्'? Observe संराजिता, संराजितुम्, संराजितव्यम् ॥

The क्विप् is added by [[3.2.61]], the ज् is changed to ष् by [[8.2.36]], which is changed to ट् at the end of a word, in संम्राट् ॥ साम्राज्यम् is formed by ष्यञ् affix, as it belongs to <i>Brahmanadi</i> class.
