ष् is the substitute of a विसर्ग when is preceded by इ or उ and is followed by an affix beginning with a hard guttural or a labial.
The word अपदादौ is understood here also. The affixes meant are the same पाश, कल्प, क, and काम्य ॥ Thus सर्पिष्पाशम्, यजुष्पाशम्, सर्पिष्कल्पम्, यजुष्कल्पम्, सर्पिष्कम्, यजुष्कम्; सर्पिष्काम्यति, यजुष्काम्यति ॥

Why do we say 'by an affix'? Observe अग्निः करोति, वायुः करोति, अग्निः पचति, वायुः पचति ॥

The affix should begin with a guttural or a labial. Therefore not here, सर्पिस्ते, यजुस्ते ॥

In the succeeding <i>sutras</i>, the <i>anuvritti</i> of स from [[8.3.39]] and of इणः षः from this, are both current. The <i>visarga</i> will be changed to ष if preceded by इ or उ, otherwise it will be स ॥

According to some, this <i>sutra</i> ordains ष् in the room of the स् taught in the preceding <i>sutra</i>, and not of <i>visarga</i> : and so also in the following <i>sutras</i>.
