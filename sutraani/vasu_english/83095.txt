ष् is substituted for the स् of स्थिरः after the words गवि  and  युधि।
Thus गविष्ठिर, युधिष्ठिर ॥ The compounding takes place by [[2.1.44]]. and the 7th case affix is not elided by [[6.3.9]]. though the word गो does not end in a consonant, yet it retains its Locative ending by virtue of this <i>sutra</i>.
