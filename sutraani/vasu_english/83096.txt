ष् is substituted for the स् of स्थल after वि , कु , शमि  and परि।
Thus विष्ठलम्, कुष्ठलम्, शमिष्ठलम् and परिष्टलम् ॥ The word वि, कु and परि are compounded under [[2.2.18]]; the word शमी forms a genitive by [[6.3.63]]. In the aphorism the word शमि is shown with a short इ, indicating thereby that when the इ is not shortened, the cerebral change does not take place; as शमीस्थलम्
