The न् of निस् , निक्ष and निन्द is changed to a ण optionally, when preceded by an उपसर्ग having in it a cause of change.
Thus प्रणिंसनम् or प्रनिंसनम्, प्रणिक्षणम् or प्रनिक्षणम्, प्रणिन्दनम् or प्रनिन्दनम् ॥

These verbs are written with ण in the <i>Dhatupatha</i>, and therefore by rule [[8.4.14]] <i>ante</i>, the change of न into ण would have been obligatory. The present <i>sutra</i> makes it optional. The root णिसि means 'to kiss', णिक्षि रोषणे, and णिदि कुत्सायाम् ॥
