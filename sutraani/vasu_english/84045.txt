In the room of a यर् letter (every consonant except ह final) in a पद , when Nasal follows, there is optionally a Nasal substitute.
The word पदान्त is understood here. Thus वाक् + नयति = वाग्नयति or वाङ्नयति, अग्निचिद्नयति or अग्निचिन्नयति, त्रिष्टुब्नयति or त्रिष्टुम्ननयति, श्वलिण्नयति or श्वलिड्नयति ॥

Why do we say 'final in a <i>Pada</i>'? Observe, वेद् + मि = वेद्मि ॥ Here there is no option. So also क्षुभ्नाति ॥

Vart:- When it is a <i>pratyaya</i> or affix that follows, the nasalisation is obligatory in the secular language. Thus वाक् + मात्रम् = वाङ्मात्रम्, कियन्मात्रम् ॥ It is, however, only before the affixes मय and मात्र that the change is obligatory, and not before every affix beginning with a nasal.
