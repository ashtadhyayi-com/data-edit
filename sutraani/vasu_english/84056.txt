The चर् is optionally the substitute of a झल् that occurs in a Pause.
The words झलां चर् is understood in the <i>sutra</i>. A sonant or a surd non-aspirate may stand as final in a Pause: but not an aspirate consonant. By [[8.2.39]], a non-aspirate sonant can only stand in a final position. This ordains that a non-aspirate surd may also stand as the final, when there is Pause. Thus वाक् or वाग्, त्वक् or त्वग्, श्वलिट् or श्वलिड्, त्रिष्टुप् or त्रिष्टुब् ॥
